# Comp Titles

**Status.** Working list — refine to 5–6 for the final proposal.

**Purpose.** Anchor Commons Bonds in the agent's mental map. Each comp title shows: there is a market for this register; here's how Commons Bonds is similar; here's how it advances the conversation.

## Working list

### Tier 1 — lead with these (Mazzucato + Pistor pair)

1. **Mariana Mazzucato — *The Value of Everything* (2018)**
   *Similar:* renames a category in economic vocabulary; trade-press accessible; non-partisan-readable.
   *Different:* Mazzucato critiques value-extraction at the level of GDP and rent; Commons Bonds operationalizes a pricing apparatus (RCV) for the costs that extraction severs.

2. **Katharina Pistor — *The Code of Capital* (2019)**
   *Similar:* institutional/legal frame; scholarly-trade crossover; introduces a formal mechanism (legal coding).
   *Different:* Pistor traces how law produces capital; Commons Bonds traces how extraction produces severed cost — and prices it.

### Tier 2 — supporting comps

3. **Mariana Mazzucato — *Mission Economy* (2021)** — solutions register; demonstrates Mazzucato readership re-buys.
4. **Brett Christophers — *The Price is Wrong* (2024)** — pricing/cost mechanics; recent (signals current appetite).
5. **Daniel Susskind — *Growth: A Reckoning* (2024)** — big-idea econ register; recent.
6. **Saskia Sassen — *Expulsions* (2014)** — moral framing; cross-disciplinary readability.
7. **Raj Patel & Jason W. Moore — *A History of the World in Seven Cheap Things* (2017)** — hidden-cost frame; narrative-driven.
8. **Dani Rodrik — *Straight Talk on Trade* (2017)** — non-partisan policy posture; agent-and-publisher familiarity.

## Selection rule for the final proposal

Cite 5–6, not 8. Lead with Mazzucato + Pistor. Keep one recent (Christophers or Susskind) to signal current market. Keep one narrative-driven (Patel/Moore) to signal voice range. Drop the rest unless an agent has a specific tie.

## What each comp does for the pitch

- **Mazzucato** = vocabulary precedent (renaming an economic concept publicly; demonstrating cross-spectrum reach)
- **Pistor** = formal-mechanism precedent (introducing a structural apparatus; scholarly-trade crossover)
- **Christophers / Susskind** = market-recency proof
- **Sassen / Patel & Moore** = voice and narrative-range proof
- **Rodrik** = non-partisan-publisher comfort signal
