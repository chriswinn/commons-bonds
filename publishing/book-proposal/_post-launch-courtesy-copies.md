# Post-launch courtesy-copy queue

**Purpose:** Subjects + named scholars + interview-declined contacts who should receive a courtesy copy of *Commons Bonds* (or the relevant derivative essay) after publication. This is a relationship-hygiene queue, not a consent or notification gate — it fires post-publication, not pre-publication.

**Discipline source:** Standard publishing practice (named subjects + key cited scholars + interview-declined contacts who left the door open for a copy) crossed with the named-subject consent discipline ([`tools/memory/feedback_named_subject_consent.md`](../../tools/memory/feedback_named_subject_consent.md)) and the named-subject consent tracker ([`research/outreach/_pipeline/consent-tracker.md`](../../research/outreach/_pipeline/consent-tracker.md)).

**When to fire:** Book release date OR essay publication date, whichever applies. For book release, batch the queue into one mailing (efficient packaging + cover-note discipline). For per-essay courtesy copies, fire as each essay places.

3. **Send the courtesy copy** with a short cover note matching the posture column.
4. **Mark Status as "Sent <YYYY-MM-DD>"** after dispatch.
5. **If a recipient responds**, log the exchange in the relevant subject folder under [`research/outreach/subjects/`](../../research/outreach/subjects/) (creating one if needed).
