# Marketing Plan

**Date drafted:** 2026-05-06  ·  **Date modified:** 2026-05-06

**Status.** Skeleton — not yet drafted. Target completion: Sept–Oct 2026, after essay-cascade results begin to land.

**Purpose.** Show the publisher's marketing department a concrete launch path. Be specific; vague marketing plans get filed under "the author thinks they don't need to do this."

## Section sketch

- **Pre-publication essays.** List each placed/queued essay with venue, date, audience reach. The cascade itself *is* the marketing plan's spine.
- **Interview / quote partners.** Mazzucato, Darity Jr., Dr. Colden (where confirmed). Each named source is a potential blurb-or-foreword candidate post-acceptance.
- **Academic adoption.** Outline outreach to heterodox-econ programs and environmental-econ syllabi. Identify 8–12 named programs.
- **Cross-disciplinary readings.** Law schools (cost-severance has labor-law and environmental-law applications); philosophy programs (intergenerational ethics).
- **Speaking / events.** Identify 3–5 venues where author can appear in launch year. Hold this section for after first placement lands.

## What this is *not*

- A social-media plan (no Substack/newsletter/podcast track per scope decision).
- An author tour pitch — small first books rarely get tours.
- A self-promotion pledge. Marketing plans that promise "I'll do everything" read as inexperience.

## Update cadence

Refresh as essays place and interviews land. Each placement re-anchors the plan.
