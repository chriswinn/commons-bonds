### §2.1.4 MEDIUM precision flag — WVBLA "led by" vs inventory "and"

Moore March 1969) and helped force the federal Coal Mine Health and Safety Act (December 1969) were rank-and-file actions organized through and supported by WVBLA. "Led by" is historically defensible per Hamby's *Soul Full of Coal Dust* + standard coal-history scholarship — but the inventory phrasing is the safer rendering.

**Hard-rule classification.** This is NOT in the rule's enumerated HARD-RULE-VIOLATION categories:

- NOT a "scene-rendered encounter" (no scene; one-line characterization).
- NOT "quoted speech" (no quotes).
- NOT a "motivation attribution to a named historical actor" (no motive characterization).
- BORDERLINE "documentary-record specific" — the rule prohibits inventing such; V-D's "led by" is not invented (defensible per historical record) but goes beyond what inventory explicitly anchors.

**Verdict.** **PASS on HARD-RULE axis — NOT a HARD-RULE-VIOLATION.** No automatic STAY LOCKED. But MEDIUM precision-adjustment opportunity flagged for author-decision spot-fix.
