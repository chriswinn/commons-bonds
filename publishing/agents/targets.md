## Discipline

- Verify each agent is open to queries and not on hiatus before adding.
- Note any known co-agent / boutique-vs-major preferences.
- Flag agents who explicitly accept simultaneous queries vs. those who require exclusivity (track in `query-tracker.md`).

## Ranking rule

- **Priority A (~8):** strongest fit + active in the comp-title sales register.
- **Priority B (~8):** good fit, slightly broader register.
- **Priority C (~9):** wildcards / narrative-nonfiction-with-systems-thinking / memoir-hybrid.

Send queries in waves matching priority — A first, B second, C third.
