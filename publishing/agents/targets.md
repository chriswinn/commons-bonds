# Agent Targets

**Status.** Skeleton — populate by Sept–Oct 2026 per cascade plan.

**Purpose.** Ranked target list of literary agents who place trade nonfiction with academic credibility — the kind who sell to Penguin Press, FSG, Knopf, PublicAffairs, Norton, Princeton Trade, Riverhead, Metropolitan/Holt.

## Sourcing approach

- Mine acknowledgments pages of comp titles (`02_comp-titles.md`) — agents are routinely thanked.
- Cross-check Publishers Marketplace deals tagged "big idea / economics" 2023–2026.
- Look up US agents for Mazzucato, Pistor, Brett Christophers, Adam Tooze, Branko Milanović.
- **Avoid:** pure-literary agents, pure-academic agents, political-memoir agents.

- ~25% narrative-nonfiction-with-systems-thinking

## Discipline

- Verify each agent is open to queries and not on hiatus before adding.
- Note any known co-agent / boutique-vs-major preferences.
- Flag agents who explicitly accept simultaneous queries vs. those who require exclusivity (track in `query-tracker.md`).

## Ranking rule

- **Priority A (~8):** strongest fit + active in the comp-title sales register.
- **Priority B (~8):** good fit, slightly broader register.
- **Priority C (~9):** wildcards / narrative-nonfiction-with-systems-thinking / memoir-hybrid.

Send queries in waves matching priority — A first, B second, C third.
