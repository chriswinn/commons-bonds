**Purpose.** Ranked target list of literary agents who place trade nonfiction with academic credibility — the kind who sell to Penguin Press, FSG, Knopf, PublicAffairs, Norton, Princeton Trade, Riverhead, Metropolitan/Holt.

**Tier 1 heuristic (per cascade-plan-v2 §11):** agent has repped ≥2 non-partisan econ-policy big-idea trade nonfiction in last 5 years AND roster shows imprints across both liberal + conservative editorial houses (Penguin Press + FSG + Knopf + Norton lanes, not just Verso + Haymarket).

**Imprint-orientation veto:** Tier 1 agents are acceptable at query time; partisan-coded imprints at offer-acceptance time (Verso left-coded; Encounter right-coded; etc.) require explicit author sign-off against FPD v1.0.0. Discipline operates at offer-acceptance, not query-send.

## Sourcing approach

- Mine acknowledgments pages of comp titles (`02_comp-titles.md`) — agents are routinely thanked.
- Cross-check Publishers Marketplace deals tagged "big idea / economics" 2023–2026.
- Look up US agents for Mazzucato, Pistor, Brett Christophers, Adam Tooze, Branko Milanović.
- **Avoid:** pure-literary agents, pure-academic agents, political-memoir agents.

## Mix target (when 25 names compiled)

- ~60% econ/policy big-idea
- ~25% narrative-nonfiction-with-systems-thinking
- ~15% wildcards (memoir-hybrid — Ch 1 is memoiristic)
