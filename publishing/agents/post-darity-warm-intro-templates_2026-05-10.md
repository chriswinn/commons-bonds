# Post-Darity warm-intro templates

**Date drafted:** 2026-05-10. **Trigger:** Sandy Darity interview 2026-05-12. **Activation rule:** if Darity engages substantively with the framework AND surfaces a name (or accepts the open-question prompt with a recommendation), activate the matching template variation within ~5 min post-call.

**Source decisions:** comp-titles deep matrix Lead 6 (`publishing/book-proposal/02_comp-titles.md`); cross-thread-todos #2 reframe (Mullen-specific → post-Darity warm-intro discovery).

**Posture.** Conversational professional. Short. Concrete. The ask is for a 30-min intro conversation OR a paragraph-blurb consideration — not for an immediate book endorsement. Always make it easy for Darity to forward verbatim.

**One-message-for-Darity rule.** Each template comes in two versions:
- **(A) Forwarding email Darity sends to the target** — what the recipient sees first.
- **(B) Direct email from author to target** — sent if Darity declines forwarding but offers permission to mention his name.

## Activation checklist (post-call)

1. **Within 1 hour of call ending:** while Darity-conversation context is fresh, decide which template variation(s) Darity surfaced.
2. **Customize TARGET_HOOK:** the most important variable — replace generic placeholders with the specific framing Darity used in the conversation.
3. **Send Darity the (A) forwarding email first** for his approval-and-forward — never CC the target before Darity has cleared the introduction.
4. **Wait 24-48h** for Darity's forward + target reply.
5. **If no reply at 1 week:** send (B) direct email with explicit "Sandy suggested I reach out" attribution, only after confirming with Darity that direct outreach is appropriate.

## What NOT to do

- **Don't activate Mullen template just because the prep work was originally Mullen-specific.** Activate based on what Darity actually surfaces.
- **Don't bcc / cc multiple targets at once.** Each variation goes to one person.
- **Don't activate Coates** — see the FHTE-blurb-absence note in `02_comp-titles.md` Lead 6 pre-interview leverage flag #3. Use the tactful Coates-blurb-question separately to learn more before any Coates ask.
- **Don't lead with the book-blurb ask.** The first ask is a 30-minute conversation. Blurb consideration is downstream; pushing too early reads as transactional.
