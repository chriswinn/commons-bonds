# Phenomenal World — submission norms

> **Firewall (§6.1):** drafter-safe block may go to a generation prompt; the editorial-only block (max + calibration band) is for Cut + Stage 5 only.

## Drafter-safe block

- **What it is:** nonprofit journal of **political economy** (Jain Family Institute). Publishes "the best in political economy from around the globe" — rigorous research and writing in **economics, history, finance, policy, politics, philosophy, sociology, economic history** and kindred fields, from new and established scholars/researchers/writers.
- **Editorial brain:** rewards **rigorous, heterodox-fluent political-economy argument** with named institutions, concrete cases, and engagement with contemporary debates *by name* (e.g., Stern–Nordhaus discount-rate, social cost of carbon). Long editorial process; values depth. Also takes analysis that **responds quickly to unfolding events.**
- **Dispositive audience (per the $100 Barrel Stage-0 carry-forward):** the **center-right policy reader is a Tier-1 gating character** — the non-partisan-framing test is dispositive. PW is left-of-center, but the framework's "architecture, not a political program" close must land for the center-right reader too (Condition-1 discipline).
- **Format:** **pitch by email** (a few paragraphs on the idea + the writer's background) OR a complete draft. Long-form essays.
- **Register:** heterodox-econ-fluent without academic-jargon density; political-economy framing; named cases + named debates.

## Editorial-only block (Cut + Stage 5 only)

- **No published hard word limit** (PW runs long-form essays; their range is broad). The $100 Barrel project target is **~3,500–5,000w** (per the brief). Treat that as the working band; PW will run longer when substance carries it.
- **Calibration band:** to establish once we have a published-length sample; for now, the ~3,500–5,000w project band is the target.
