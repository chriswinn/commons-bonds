## 2. Editorial brain map for Noema specifically

What Noema editors *want* (and what Aeon doesn't equally demand):

- **Three-register weave** (memoir + history/theory + reportage) — not two. Aeon tolerates memoir-plus-thought-experiment; Noema requires reportage substance.
- **Named living sources** with cited public-record authority. Concrete numbers, specific institutions, dated quotes.
- **Internationalist scope.** Not US-only. Norway / Mondragon / global sourcing required.
- **Cultural-engagement seriousness.** Berggruen Institute prizes engagement with non-Western intellectual lineages or with US Black-literary tradition. The Close's Dunbar/Du Bois/Fanon/Ellison passage is on-brand.
- **Length discipline.** 2,500–4,000 words is the stated band. Pieces published over 4,000 are by editorial assignment, not unsolicited submission. Target ≤ 4,000 with breathing room.
- **Three-word subheadings** preferred. Hyperlinks on factual references (1–2 words per link). Image suggestions. AI-collaboration disclosure paragraph in cover note or footer.

What hurts Noema submissions specifically (vs. Aeon):

- Pure thought-experiment (Noema wants real-world stake).
- No named living source (reportage register requires it).
- Apparatus jargon ("cluster-γ," RCV-as-acronym, etc.). Editorial brain is general-intellectual, not academic.
- Western-philosophy-only frame.
- Throat-clearing (daydreamer-tells-don't-show, "I have used my entire career," nostalgia-for-people-who-don't-exist-anymore).
- Defensive author register (preemptive-rebuttal-of-objections at length).

## 5. Voice register (ratified)

- First person, plain, unsentimental.
- Short paragraphs OK but vary sentence length within them. Counter the declarative-three-in-a-row LLM cadence by mixing 5-word and 25-word sentences.
- Italicize remembered direct speech and key terms (em-dash + italics for spoken lines).
- Avoid LLM tics: rule of three, em-dash crutches, tidy parallels, "in short" / "ultimately" connective tissue, "It changed me. It humbled me. It made..." cadence, "There are not many people like that anymore" nostalgia.
- Specific concrete details over abstract claims. The handoff doc principle: "recliner, drill bits, fifty-eight countries."
- Read each paragraph aloud before showing.
- Drop the apparatus. No "RCV," no "cluster-γ," no acronyms, no formal three-component breakdowns. The framework's load-bearing claim is *cost severance is the structural mechanism by which value is captured by one party while costs are absorbed by another who cannot bill back* — that's the only definition the essay needs.

### 7.4 Section IV / V framework + cited research

- **Cost severance defined** in plain language (no acronyms, no formal apparatus). The framework's only load-bearing claim for the essay: cost severance is the structural mechanism by which extraction value is captured by one party while costs are absorbed by another who cannot bill back.
- **Libby, Montana** — W. R. Grace vermiculite, ~$100M revenue captured, billions in asbestos disease costs, federal public-health-emergency designation. ~40-to-1 cost-to-revenue ratio.
- **2008 financial crisis** — origination fees captured during the boom; foreclosure losses, retirement collapses, generational household-wealth hit fell on the people who bought the products.
- **Norway 1969 / Ekofisk → 1990 Government Petroleum Fund (renamed Government Pension Fund Global)** — ~$1.9T total / ~$340K per Norwegian citizen. Fund captures rents through state licenses, taxation, direct ownership of state operating company; invested outside Norway to avoid distorting domestic economy. 2001 fiscal rule — held by every government since regardless of coalition — caps annual government spending at the expected real return on the fund (initially set at ~4% in 2001; lowered to ~3% in 2017 as long-run return expectations declined). Capital itself inviolate.
- **Venezuela / Nigeria** as comparison cases (cost severance not reduced).
- **Darity & Mullen, *From Here to Equality* (2020)** — cumulative wealth deficit between Black and white US households accounting for slavery, sharecropping, redlining, convict leasing, discriminatory homeownership policy, educational/employment exclusion. ~$14T figure.
- **Hamilton, Darity, Price, Sridharan, Tippett, *Umbrellas Don't Make It Rain* (2015)** — Black households at highest measurable achievement strata (college-educated, dual-income, married) display median wealth substantially below white households at lower achievement strata. The umbrella does not make the rain.

### 7.5 Section VI Mazzucato + Mondragon

- **Mariana Mazzucato, *The Value of Everything*** — value creation (produces something genuinely new) vs. value extraction (moves existing value into a concentrator without producing new wealth). Examples: financier bundling mortgages into securities (extraction, not creation); pharmaceutical firm acquiring a drug and raising the price from $13.50 to $750 (Daraprim case, Turing Pharmaceuticals 2015; extraction; the drug already existed, what the firm captures is rent).
- **Healthcare cost severance** — $6,000 pacemaker vs. ~$86 reimbursement under Medicare's advance-care-planning code (CPT 99497, 2024 RVU schedule) for the conversation about whether the implant is the right call; downstream cost severance: unpaid caregiving labor of the spouse, the patient living for years with a device that should have been declined, family ruin when treatment crosses where insurance ends.
- **Mondragon** — Father José María Arizmendiarrieta founded the federation in 1956 in the Basque region of Spain; 70 years across multiple economic cycles including the European debt crisis. ~95 worker-owned cooperatives, ~70,000 workers (2023 Mondragon annual report figure; historic peak ~92K pre-Fagor closure). Every employee (after probation) holds full ownership stake and a vote regardless of position. Pay ratio capped institutionally — historically near 3:1, widening in largest industrial cooperatives to 6–9:1. Fortune 500 comparison ~300:1. Caja Laboral bank (founded 1959) lets capital move between cooperatives without private-equity layer. 2013 Fagor flagship appliance manufacturer became unviable; workers absorbed into other cooperatives; federation survived the failure.
