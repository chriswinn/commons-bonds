## 8. Why this experiment exists

The two-stage drafting discipline (Stage 1 pre-draft audience pass + Stage 2 audience-blind flow draft + Stage 3 post-flow rigor pass) was identified as a candidate best practice mid-session on 2026-05-09. The first test (Noema essay) is in flight. This is the second test, with two purposes:

1. **Cross-validate the discipline across artifact types and venues.** A 300-word pitch and a 3,800-word essay are very different artifacts. If the discipline produces consistent improvements across both, that's robust evidence. If results diverge between tests, we learn something about the discipline's domain of applicability.

2. **Comparison against a *ratified* control.** Pitch A (Version C) was ratified through a deliberate process including the title rigor pass v1.0.0 — it's a strong control. Beating Pitch A would be meaningful evidence the discipline adds value even compared to careful conventional process.
