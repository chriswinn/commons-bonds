## §1. Pass 3.3 + 3.4 scope reminder + variant definitions

### §1.1 What β changes from α (V-D)

Two word-level subtractive trims; no factual claims introduced; no semantic
shift in argument:

- **¶3 sentence 1.** V-D *"What follows is a pricing framework, built
  outside the academy across a decade. It names patterns through which
  costs get hidden: ..."* → β *"A pricing framework, built outside the
  academy across a decade, names patterns through which costs get hidden:
  ..."* Saves 4 words by dropping the forward-reference *"What follows is..."*
  and the pronoun resumption *"It names..."* in favor of a single-sentence
  participial restructure. V-D INDEPENDENT-AUDIT §2 T1.1 explicitly
  acknowledged this as a residual very-mild structural self-reference
  *"below Aeon disqualifier threshold"* — β removes the residual.
- **¶4 sentence 1.** V-D *"The central claim is that the framework is
  universal: a pricing framework first, an analytical lens second."* → β
  *"The framework is universal: a pricing framework first, an analytical
  lens second."* Saves 5 words by dropping the meta-phrase *"The central
  claim is that..."*. Same Stage 1 §2 anti-pattern axis: this is announce-
  the-move ("here comes the central claim") rather than make-the-move
  (state it).

Word delta: 298w → 289w (−9w). Em-dash count unchanged (4 chars / 3 usages,
same sites). Load-bearing claims preserved verbatim.

### §1.2 What γ changes from β

One word-level subtractive trim; no factual claims introduced:

- **¶1 sentence 2.** β *"At the airlock, you are asked how you will be
  paying for your air."* → γ *"At the airlock, you pay for your air."*
  Saves 6 words by switching the reveal device from question-as-
  confrontation (someone at the airlock poses the payment question to you)
  to act-of-payment-as-recognition (you perform the payment yourself).

  **Two effects this introduces:**

  (a) **Internal-consistency gain.** V-D / V-C / β all set up the airlock
  moment as a hypothetical question and then move to actual-mode at V-D's
  terminal *"lands in arithmetic"* close. γ holds the whole pitch in actual-
  mode (you pay → costs are paid → framework names patterns → math is done
  → arithmetic). The pitch's claim-arc is structurally tighter.

  (b) **Aeon-house-voice trade.** Stage 1 §2 specifies *"thought-experiment
  register"* as a core Aeon attribute. The canonical thought-experiment
  construction in Aeon's lineage (Mulgan / MacAskill / Parfit) often uses
  question-frame ("you are asked," "imagine that you must decide," "suppose
  you encounter"). Action-frame ("you pay") is still under the *"Imagine
  landing"* hypothetical umbrella from sentence 1, so γ does not exit
  thought-experiment register entirely — but the canonical-construction
  signal is weakened by one notch.

Word delta vs β: 289w → 283w (−6w). Vs V-D / α: 298w → 283w (−15w).
Em-dash count unchanged (sites unchanged). Load-bearing claims preserved
verbatim.
