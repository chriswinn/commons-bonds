### The MUST-NOW add: Nozick entitlement + the Lockean proviso turned in the book's favor

**Why it lands.** The objection a Nozickian raises is: pricing the commons and
posting a bond is a *taking* — a coercive redistribution that violates
just-acquisition entitlement. The framework's winning answer (verification §2 #4)
is that the objection **turns against itself**: Nozick's own theory imports the
**Lockean proviso** ("enough, and as good, left in common for others"), and
permanent foreclosure of a non-renewable resource is the textbook case where the
proviso is *violated* — there is precisely NOT enough-and-as-good left for the
people downstream and the people not yet born. The framework can therefore claim
**Nozick's own proviso as its license**: pricing the foreclosed residual is what
restores the entitlement chain the proviso requires, not a taking that breaks it.
This is the strongest possible center-right disarm because it is won on the
libertarian's own terms, not against them.

**Where it slots (no new section needed).** L82 already pivots to "a tradition
that comes at all of this from a different political direction [Public Choice]
arrives at a usefully compatible place." The Nozick/Lockean-proviso move is the
natural companion right-flank paragraph adjacent to L82 (or folded into the
"## The politics of resistance" → "## What already works" seam). It mirrors the
existing Coase efficiency-objection handling (L16) on the *rights* axis the way
Coase handles the *efficiency* axis — completing a symmetry the essay already
half-builds.

**Sizing.** SMALL-UPDATE: ~1 paragraph. The structure is: (1) name the
libertarian rights objection (this is a taking); (2) name Nozick's entitlement
theory and its embedded Lockean proviso; (3) turn it — permanent foreclosure
violates "enough and as good left for others," so honest pricing *restores* the
entitlement the proviso demands rather than breaching it. Plain-language; no
apparatus; no coercion/reparations dollar figure (the Darity abstention at L80
already governs and is untouched).
