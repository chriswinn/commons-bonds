## 0. Orientation — what this document IS

The Technical Appendix is the book's **academic-reviewer backstop**: the place where every claim the chapters make at dinner-table register gets its formal definition, proof, lineage citation, sensitivity analysis, and worked calibration. It is not end-user-facing prose in the chapter sense; its audience is the economist/reviewer who asks "is this rigorous, and is it yours?" Its job is **defensibility**, not persuasion. That reframes the whole cut analysis below: the bar for cutting is not "does the general reader need this?" but "does a hostile reviewer need this to be convinced the apparatus is sound and honestly attributed?" Much of what would be ruthless overload in a chapter is load-bearing here. The memo flags the genuine excess — but the dominant finding is not bloat. It is a **positioning hole** (Polanyi/Fraser absence) and a **figure-drift defect** (McDowell carbon term) that a reviewer will hit before they ever notice length.

## 1. The chapter's irreducible job / spine

**Spine (one sentence):** *Prove that for non-renewable / commodified-commons extraction under current institutions Cost Severance is structurally positive (CS = RCV − B > 0), give the apparatus that measures RCV honestly and conservatively, and attribute every component to prior art so the contribution reads as configuration, not invention.*

The irreducible chain, in dependency order:

1. **Primitives + central equation** (§1): R, S(t), U, E, D, RCV integral, `CS = RCV − B`. Everything downstream is unusable without this.
2. **The discrimination + admission apparatus** (§6 CIT + §7 Four Gates): what is allowed into the RCV integrand, and why that is not "choose-your-own-adventure." This is the falsifiability/anti-overcounting backbone.
3. **The measurement triangulation** (§3 Three Ways: M1 replacement / M2 revealed preference / M3 scarcity-adjusted option value): conservative-by-construction, no single-method claim has standing.
4. **The structural theorems** (§10.1a / §10.1b): CS > 0 follows from Coase-extension (P1) + Pigou-extension (P2) + empirical inadequacy (P3).
5. **The bidirectional account** (§2 + §5: CSD↔B₁ / RCV↔B₂; `total CS = (CSD−B₁) + (RCV−B₂)`): backward Restitution + forward Foreclosure.
6. **The bridge to canonical resource economics** (§4 Hotelling Identity: `RCV − Hotelling rent = CS`).
7. **The decision rule under irreversibility** (§8 ARR) + its estimation companion (§3.5 / §11.8 α-dominance).
8. **Empirical calibration** (§11: McDowell + Norway as the two executed Three-Ways cases; four case prefaces; convergence table).
9. **Honest lineage + limits** (§14 lit-engagement; §15 methodology-defense + limitations; §16/§17 extensions + generalization).

Everything in the TA maps onto one of these nine. Nothing in the spine is dispensable. The cut analysis (§3 below) operates entirely on **depth, redundancy, and meta-commentary within** these nine, not on whole-limb amputation.
