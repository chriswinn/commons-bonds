## (a) Contribution-matrix cells + meta-claims: what is explicit, how well positioned

### META-CLAIM 1 — Structural-commons / fictitious-commodity (Polanyi/Fraser)
**Status: PARTIALLY MADE, IMPLICIT, NOT NAMED. This is the essay's single biggest positioning gap — and it is the portfolio's biggest gap, so the essay reproduces it.**

- The essay *demonstrates* the same-object claim operationally: reef/atmosphere (Deepwater, climate), community (Libby, McDowell), labor (black lung), future generation (Social Security, climate) are all run through ONE mechanism — cost severance. The "the mechanism is the same" close to §"The pattern across cases" is the meta-claim in action.
- But Polanyi and Fraser are **entirely absent**. The phrase "fictitious commodity" never appears. The essay never says "these are the same object — reef, atmosphere, community, labor, future generation — that markets treat as inputs but that are not produced for sale." It leaves the deepest unifying claim as an emergent property the reader must infer rather than a named contribution.
- Consequence: the essay's top move (framework PRICES + RESERVES the commodification the diagnostic tradition only NAMED) is half-stated. The "prices" half is vivid (40:1, $150B, $16T). The "what gets commodified and why these cases rhyme" half — the Polanyi structural anchor — is left on the table. Christophers ("the prices ... are products of how the code is written") is the closest the essay comes, but Christophers is a price-formation point, not the fictitious-commodity point.
- **Positioning quality: weak.** This is a contradict/extend-the-diagnostic-tradition move, and the essay does not stage it as such. There is no "Polanyi named this; Fraser extended it to social reproduction; I price and reserve it" sentence anywhere.

### META-CLAIM 2 — Configurational novelty (integrated apparatus extending Pigou + Ostrom into extraction)
**Status: PARTIALLY MADE. Two of the integrated config's instruments are fully present and well-built; the apparatus-as-configuration claim is RESERVED (correctly).**

- Present and strong: **Two Instruments** (Restitution Bond + Foreclosure Bond) get full sections, mechanism, worked application, and a careful name-disambiguation (contractor-bond family vs. saver-bond). This is the configurational core the essay chooses to expose.
- Present implicitly: the **backward/forward bidirectionality** (the Three Ways / Two Directions structure) is explicit and well-staged — "the accountability gap, properly seen, is bidirectional."
- Hotelling enters via Hartwick (the Hartwick rule is named and dated 1977; Hotelling is the implicit parent but not named). Non-renewable foreclosure is *demonstrated* (petroleum runs out; Norway funds the future) but the Hotelling identity / S_max ≤ 1 generalization is RESERVED.
- Reserved (correctly, for the book): CIT (the Cost Internalization Theorem), the Four Gates, the Three Ways as a named taxonomy, the Hotelling Identity, the ARR, the formula. The essay never claims "I have an integrated framework"; it presents two instruments and a diagnosis. **This is good reserve discipline** — the apparatus stays in the book.
- **Positioning quality: good on what it exposes, but the "extends Pigou + Ostrom into extraction settings" claim is not staged.** Ostrom is absent by name; Pigou is absent by name. The essay never says "Pigou priced the externality but assumed B = the cost; here B < RCV" or "Ostrom solved the renewable commons; non-renewable foreclosure is the unsolved case." The extension claims are *enacted* but not *positioned*.

### META-CLAIM 3 — Measurement, not new diagnosis; conservative by construction
**Status: STRONGLY AND EXPLICITLY MADE. The essay's best-positioned meta-claim.**

- The "measures the mechanism the diagnostic tradition only diagnosed" move is the explicit spine of §"Why the existing architecture cannot reach this." Pistor diagnoses the code; public-choice (Buchanan/Tullock) diagnoses why it persists; "**Cost-severance accounting does not replace this analysis. It prices what the rent-seeking buys.**" — this is the add-magnitude-to-an-existing-diagnosis contribution stated cleanly. Same move on Christophers and on Darity/Mullen ("the claim that taught the method how to count").
- Conservative-by-construction is **demonstrated but under-named.** The essay repeatedly uses "documented costs," "at least forty," "floor," "the honest target is the full hundred" — all conservative-framing moves. But it does not name the construction (Weitzman dismal-theorem downward bias; CS > 0 across the Nordhaus–Stern discount-rate spread; non-aligned-by-design). Weitzman, Nordhaus, Stern are all absent. The essay earns the conservatism in prose without claiming it as a methodological property.
- **Positioning quality: strong on "measurement not diagnosis"; moderate on "conservative by construction."** The add/extend relationships to Pistor, public-choice, Christophers, and Darity are all EXPLICIT and well-staged — these are the cleanest positioned cells in the essay.

9. **"What is engineered can be engineered differently."** — clean thesis-restatement for the book's pivot from diagnosis to instrument.
