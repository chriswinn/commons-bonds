## (a) Contribution-matrix cells + meta-claims made explicit, and positioning quality

This is an op-ed, deliberately apparatus-stripped (0 named framework terms in both versions, by design). So it carries the matrix **implicitly** — it dramatizes one cell rather than positioning the framework against the literature. Read against the three meta-claims:

**META-CLAIM 1 — STRUCTURAL-COMMONS / FICTITIOUS-COMMODITY (Polanyi/Fraser): PARTIALLY CARRIED, IMPLICIT.**
The LEAD's strongest move is exactly the fictitious-commodity intuition rendered in plain English: the atmosphere "does not bill in advance"; the carbon, the illness, and "the ruined water flowed to a real creek in a real place"; the county itself was drawn against — "a loan, drawn silently against the future and against the county itself." This is Polanyi's land/labor-as-fictitious-commodity made concrete across atmosphere + labor (black lung) + community (the county) + future generation ("against the future"). The LEAD even gestures at the SAME-OBJECT claim ("The point is the column" — all the unbooked costs are one ledger entry). But it is dramatized, not named: no Polanyi, no Fraser, no "fictitious commodity," and the framework's distinctive move (it **prices + reserves** what the diagnostic tradition only named) is absent — the op-ed prices honestly but stops at "stop pretending the costs are zero" without the reserve/restitution instrument. The ratified top move + biggest gap (Polanyi/Fraser fill) is therefore **gestured but un-banked** here, which is correct for an op-ed but means this artifact contributes nothing to closing the matrix's biggest gap.

**META-CLAIM 2 — CONFIGURATIONAL NOVELTY (CIT, Four Gates, Three Ways, Two Instruments, Hotelling, ARR): NOT CARRIED (by design).**
None of the integrated configuration surfaces. The op-ed carries only the single externality-pricing intuition (the Pigou extend: B < RCV, "whoever creates a cost should bear it"). No Hotelling foreclosure, no Ostrom-renewable-vs-extraction distinction, no ARR/Restitution instrument. The non-renewable-foreclosure dimension — coal as a thing once burned cannot un-burn, "gave up its future one seam at a time" — is *present as image* but not as the Hotelling/extraction-setting extension of Pigou+Ostrom. The configurational-novelty claim is wholly reserved.

**META-CLAIM 3 — MEASUREMENT NOT NEW DIAGNOSIS, CONSERVATIVE BY CONSTRUCTION: CARRIED IMPLICITLY, POSITIONING WEAK.**
The LEAD's whole argument is measurement-over-diagnosis: "blame is not the point... The point is the column"; "The first honest act is not a policy. It is an accounting." That is exactly the "we add magnitude, not a new diagnosis" posture (vs Harvey/Mazzucato — measures the mechanism the diagnostic tradition named). But the *conservative-by-construction* leg (Weitzman biases down; CS > 0 across Nordhaus–Stern; non-aligned) is absent in the LEAD and only weakly present in op-ed.md (the SCC-revision-history paragraph shows the number is contested and the framework adopts the mainstream EPA figure rather than the highest available — an implicit conservatism, but not stated as "conservative by construction"). The non-partisan leg IS explicit and well-positioned in both: LEAD's "That principle does not belong to the left or the right... the premise under every functioning market and every theory of personal responsibility" is the single best non-aligned articulation in the corpus.

**Positioning verdict:** This is a one-cell dramatization, not a positioned contribution. The add/parallel/contradict/extend relationships to the literature are entirely invisible (appropriate for an op-ed). The Pigou-extend (B < RCV) is the only matrix relationship present, and it is rendered as folk-wisdom ("whoever creates a cost should bear it") rather than positioned against Pigou. **Summary, not positioning** — which is the correct register for the venue but means the artifact is a demand-generation instrument, not a contribution-stake.

4. **Tighten the SAME-OBJECT claim.** "The point is the column" is excellent but the reader has to assemble the reef/atmosphere/labor/community/future equivalence themselves. One explicit line that the poisoned creek, the scarred lung, the emptied town, and the loaded atmosphere are *the same unbooked entry* would land Meta-claim 1's structural-commons move without Polanyi's name.
