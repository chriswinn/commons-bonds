Voice-flow: technically smooth. This is the single substantive Pass 3.5 finding I'd surface.

**F-FRESH-3.5-1 (MEDIUM) — §IV ¶3 added bridge cadence-tunable.**

The Condition 1 disarming work at §IV ¶3 is load-bearing and should NOT be removed. The phrasing "Let me be precise about what I am doing with him" can be tightened without losing the disarming work.

*Options:*
- **A. PRESERVE-AS-IS in V-D.** The bridge does the throat-clearing work that recovers F-VP-Barrel-4 Option D's drop. Phrasing acceptable for the dispositive-axis lift. Risk: marginally LLM-tic-adjacent on prose-craft axis.

- **C. Alternative: *"That is Rawls applied across time. One clarification first. I am using Rawls as one frame among several..."*** Tighter still; shifts the throat-clearing from "let me" register to "one clarification first" register.
- **D. Drop the bridge entirely, restore Locked.** Loses the Condition 1 dispositive-axis lift. Not recommended.

**Recommendation:** **Option A (PRESERVE-AS-IS) as default.** The throat-clearing site is the load-bearing improvement V-D makes over Locked on the Condition 1 dispositive axis. The phrasing tradeoff (slightly LLM-tic-adjacent) is below the threshold at which it would degrade any acceptance verdict; the gain on Tier 1 #3 + A4 dispositive defense is meaningful.

**Option B is a marginally-stronger polish alternative if the author wants the §III/§IV parallelism.** Options C + D should be declined.

*Reasoning:* The MEDIUM severity reflects that this is a substantive concern at the prose-craft / voice-register axis that deserves author attention. But the Condition-1-load-bearing function of the bridge is more important than the cadence cost, so PRESERVE remains the default.

*"By the figures collated in the OPEC Annual Statistical Bulletin and the U.S. Energy Information Administration's Nigeria Country Analysis Brief, the Delta has produced, over more than half a century, well in excess of thirty billion barrels of crude."*

**F-FRESH-3.5-2 (LOW) — §VI Nigeria OPEC/EIA citation prefix construction.**

The institutional-citation work is load-bearing for A1 defense + Mazzucato architecture signal. The phrasing is defensible at the cadence axis but slightly bureaucratic-cadenced.

*Options:*
- **A. PRESERVE-AS-IS in V-D.** Institutional-citation load-bearing for A1 defense; cadence cost acceptable.
- **B. Compress to: *"By OPEC and EIA figures..."*** Tightens significantly (~14w savings) but loses the formal-institutional register that A1 defense relies on (full publication titles).
