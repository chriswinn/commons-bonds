## 2. The TWO HIGH-priority GAPS

### GAP-1 — Ostrom positioning is ENTIRELY ABSENT (+ the 06-19 corrected axis is unreflected)

- **Evidence:** `grep -i ostrom` on the brief = 0 hits (the only "homogeneous" is
  inside the Norway-is-special transferability objection, a different move). The
  inherited stage-1-brief carried Ostrom as **LOAD-BEARING** (one prose mention at
  §III/§VI "if the commons-extension move surfaces" — inherited §15, L214/L524/L803).
  The redraft-brief's §3 table, which *governs where it conflicts*, silently drops it.
- **Why it's HIGH:** FA's whole argument (Norway/Nigeria; cost-bearer ≠
  value-capturer; present extraction foreclosing on absent future people) is the
  textbook **shared-fate-breaks** case the corrected axis describes. An Ostrom-
  literate FA/IPE reader is *exactly* the reader who asks "isn't this Ostrom's
  commons governance?" Under Path B + lit-positioning-primacy (the PRIMARY success
  criterion), silence means the drafter likely omits it or reaches for the
  **retired** axis. The 06-19 propagation commit (`694e2eba`) updated STATE + the
  matrix handoffs + the kickoff but **touched no FA file** — so the brief is the
  artifact still carrying the gap.
- **Tie to GAP-2:** the matrix `(b)` truth-fix (T6, **MUST-NOW**) names *both*
  Pigou and Ostrom; these two gaps are one instruction with two faces.

### GAP-2 — The narrow "what the book ADDS" contribution claim + the `(b)` guardrail are not carried as an instruction

- **Evidence:** the brief states the success criterion ("what the book supports,
  contradicts, and adds," L145) but the only place it articulates the *add* is
  obliquely, inside the Fanning-Hickel climate row ("per-extraction-event micro-
  instrument vs national-aggregate macro-accounting"). There is **no general
  framework-contribution instruction** and **no guardrail** against the broad
  version.
- **The MUST-NOW rule (matrix L86–98):** the broad "EXTENDS Pigou + Ostrom into a
  setting **NEITHER addressed**" is **FALSE and must NOT be asserted** (Hartwick
  1977 + Dasgupta priced the drawn-down resource; Ostrom's CPR work covers
  appropriation-from-depletable-stock). State **only** the **instrument-form-and-
  level** version: a per-extraction-event, bidirectional, reserve-against
  instrument, and its level. Crispest articulation (available, not required at FA):
  "Pistor names the location, Mazzucato the activity, Christophers the mechanism,
  Susskind the metric — none operationalize the corrective at the per-extraction-
  event level; what remains is the assembly."
- **Why it's HIGH:** lit-positioning-primacy makes "what it adds" the thing the FA
  reader must leave with. If the redraft articulates the *wrong* (broad) version,
  the most sophisticated FA reviewer rejects it on sight — the exact opposite of
