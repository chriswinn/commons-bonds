## §2. Per-finding dispositions

### F-1 (HIGH; MAZZUCATO REGISTER A TIGHTENING; SPOT-FIX APPLIED)

**Problem.** §III Mazzucato paragraph contained the construction: *"The mission-economy frame is generative; it has produced policy programs in several jurisdictions; it has informed the design of the European Union's Recovery and Resilience Facility, among other instruments. But the operationalization of the corrective at the transaction level... remains to be done."*

The "But... remains to be done" pivot is structurally adjacent to Stage 1 brief §10.3 banned phrase-patterns:
- ❌ "Mazzucato shows X **but stops short of** Y"
- ❌ Any "X but Y" construction across reviewed-author-name + essay's analytical move that codes as superior-judgment-of-reviewer

The Register A signal-positioning in the next sentence ("Mazzucato's diagnosis is what makes that task legible as the work it would be") rescues the construction, but the "But... remains to be done" pivot is a load-bearing tell that a careful Mazzucato reading might flag. Given Mazzucato's status as:
- **Tier 1 #4 INCLUDE-required** per Condition NR-2
- **Blurb-network candidate #1** per [`publishing/blurbs/blurb-network-register.md`](../../publishing/blurbs/blurb-network-register.md) §2 (with prior-decline 2026-05-21 raising sensitivity)
- **Closest comp-title for the framework's Ring-1 "Value Extraction"**

The tightening is warranted.

**Recommendation ratified:** Reframe to "The operationalization... is a further task the frame opens" — removes the "But" pivot; recasts the further work as something Mazzucato's frame *opens*, not as something her work fails to do.

**Spot-fix applied:**
- **Before:** "...among other instruments. But the operationalization of the corrective at the transaction level, [details], remains to be done."
- **After:** "...among other instruments. The operationalization of the corrective at the transaction level, [details], is a further task the frame opens."

Status: ✅ APPLIED 2026-05-27.

### F-2 (MED; CHRISTOPHERS VERSO-ASIDE; SPOT-FIX APPLIED)

**Problem.** §IV paragraph 73 contained: *"There is little manifesto in *The Price Is Wrong*, despite its publication by Verso, and the analytical posture is closer to engineering than to advocacy."*

The "despite its publication by Verso" aside frames Verso-press culture as polemical-by-default and Christophers as exceptional-for-Verso. While the broader sentence is positive about Christophers's analytical posture, the aside could read as:
- Condescending to Verso authors as a class (Verso editorial brain might flag)
- Subtly elevating Christophers above his publisher (which is patronizing rather than directly positive)

Christophers reading carefully might not exclude on this, but the aside introduces unnecessary register-friction with no analytical payoff. The line works better without the publisher comparison.

**Recommendation ratified:** Remove the "despite its publication by Verso" aside; the sentence is stronger without it.

**Spot-fix applied:**
- **Before:** "There is little manifesto in *The Price Is Wrong*, despite its publication by Verso, and the analytical posture is closer to engineering than to advocacy."
- **After:** "There is little manifesto in *The Price Is Wrong*; the analytical posture is closer to engineering than to advocacy."

Status: ✅ APPLIED 2026-05-27.

### F-3 (MED; PISTOR MI-3 FRAMING; SPOT-FIX APPLIED)

**Problem.** §II MI-3 acknowledgment paragraph opened with: *"A subtle limit of the book, which Pistor would be the first to acknowledge, is that the legal-modules account assumes a relative homogeneity of interest..."*

The "A subtle limit of the book" framing is structurally critique-register — "limit of the book" reads as finding-of-limitation-by-the-reviewer, even when softened by "which Pistor would be the first to acknowledge." Per Stage 1 brief §10.6, the discipline allows noting scope-limits when framed as "outside the book's frame," not as "limit of the book."

Pistor reading carefully might prefer a scope-honest reframing that positions the MI-3 observation as a question-the-book-opens rather than a limit-the-book-has.

**Recommendation ratified:** Reframe to "A scope the book leaves to further work" — positions the MI-3 observation as forward-looking scope rather than backward-looking limitation.

**Spot-fix applied:**
- **Before:** "A subtle limit of the book, which Pistor would be the first to acknowledge, is that the legal-modules account assumes a relative homogeneity of interest..."
- **After:** "A scope the book leaves to further work, which Pistor would be the first to acknowledge, is that the legal-modules account assumes a relative homogeneity of interest..."

Status: ✅ APPLIED 2026-05-27.
