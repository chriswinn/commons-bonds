### §1.1 A1 — Pure-libertarian / Mises-Institute reader

**Brief specification (§1):** "Predisposed-hostile-to-mechanism-pricing; non-essay-disarmable; reception-cycle mitigation only. The essay's non-libertarian center-right disarm (#3) handles A1-adjacent positions structurally."

**Independent read.** What A1 encounters:

- **§I coastline opener.** "The architecture, not the contamination, governs the cost-bearing distribution." A1 reads this as priming the reader to attribute outcomes to legal-state architecture rather than to natural market-process. The visual handle is striking but the analytical takeaway codes against A1's praxeological commitments.
- **§II Pistor.** "Capital is a legal artifact." A1 fundamentally rejects this — for Misesians, capital is a praxeological category (produced goods used to produce further goods), not a legal one. Pistor's *NML Capital v. Argentina* case material reads to A1 as creditor-rights-defense by sovereign-debt market discipline, NOT as the architecture-of-extraction the essay frames it as. The intergenerational-trust paragraph "structurally insulated from the political processes that might... have addressed it" reads to A1 as a feature, not a bug — wealth preservation through legal devices that protect against political confiscation IS the rule of law operating as designed.
- **§III Mazzucato value-extraction.** A1 reads this as the standard left-Marxist value-extraction trope re-warmed. The Sovaldi case material reads to A1 as misframed — Gilead acquired the molecule via a market transaction; the public-NIH-funding-share argument confuses basic-research subsidy with private commercialization risk-bearing; pricing reflects what the molecule is worth, not what its precursors cost. The asset-manager fee-extraction argument reads to A1 as anti-financialization populism.
- **§IV Christophers.** Worst section for A1. Christophers's explicit conclusion — "public ownership, public investment, public-purpose capital allocation, and regulatory restructuring" — is reported by the essay in tones the reviewer-not-architect register makes hard to distance from. The Danish/German/Spanish public-investment counterpoint reads to A1 as state-led-energy-transition advocacy.
- **§V Susskind.** A1 finds Susskind's redirection-via-revised-national-accounts politically untenable; "tax structures that price the extractive forms of growth more accurately, public investment in the forms of growth that compound human flourishing, regulatory restructurings" reads to A1 as planning-by-another-name.
- **§VII three-route close.** Partial disarmer lands: "market discipline that priced what the current pricing mechanism fails to price" is the A1-friendly pathway. The Hartwick rule + Norwegian sovereign-wealth-fund template (priced cost severance via institutional design rather than direct regulation) is the closest the essay gets to A1-friendly territory. The §VII Buchanan/Tullock acknowledgment is for A3 not A1; A1 reads Public Choice as descriptive, not prescriptive.

**Disarmer assessment.** Brief flags A1 as non-disarmable; this read confirms. The Pistor-as-legal-architecture diagnosis, Mazzucato value-extraction frame, Christophers public-investment endorsement-by-reportage, and Susskind state-led-accounting-redirection are all visceral push-points for A1. The §VII market-discipline pathway is the only partial disarmer; it doesn't land because the cumulative essay direction is opposed.

**Verdict: ⚠⚠ EXCLUDE — ACCEPTABLE LOSS BY DESIGN per Stage 1 brief §1 A1 specification.** Reception-chain weighting LOWER (A1 not in NYRB direct readership); aggregate impact priced in at Stage 1.

### §1.3 A3 — Public-Choice-only reader (Buchanan/Tullock orthodoxy)

**Brief specification (§1):** "Disarmable via the lineage-register move (Ch 5 BR brief Decision #12 precedent — credit Buchanan / Tullock as complementary lens; rent-seeking-as-architecture-shaping is on the essay's side, not against it)."

**Disarmer assessment.** Brief-prescribed disarmer lands verbatim at §VII intellectual-foundations paragraph. Public Choice is credited by name with the correct substantive position. The essay's cumulative direction remains left-of-center but the disarmer is genuine and substantial.

**Verdict: ⚠ EXCLUDE (mild) — disarmer-strong on §VII; cumulative direction codes left-of-center; close-read pulls toward ACCEPTABLE-LOSS-CLOSE-TO-INCLUDE.** Reception-chain weighting MEDIUM; disarmer effective per brief prediction.
