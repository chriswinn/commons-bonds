## CRITICAL CORRECTIONS (reputational priority — apply before submission)

1. **Line 28 — fiscal rule.** Prose reads "The fiscal rule, adopted in 2001 … roughly three percent." This is WRONG as a date/rate pairing. The rule was **introduced 2001 at 4%, lowered to 3% in 2017** (regjeringen.no). Per register §2 item 1 (also appears Ch 6:221, TA §11.5). FIX: "The fiscal rule, adopted in 2001 (initially capping withdrawals at the fund's expected 4% real return, tightened to roughly 3% in 2017) …" — or equivalent that splits the date from the current 3% rate.
2. **Line 26 — per-capita.** Prose reads "roughly three hundred and sixty thousand dollars." Now **~$390,000** (time-sensitive; NBIM end-2025). Per register §2 item 12. FIX the figure to ~$390k.
3. **Line 48 — "seventy to eighty cents of every dollar of net petroleum wealth."** Register §1E/§2 caution: the **78% figure is a combined marginal tax rate** (22% ordinary + 56% special), NOT the aggregate share of net wealth captured. As written, "70–80% of net wealth" conflates the marginal rate with the aggregate take. RECAST so the 78% reads as the marginal petroleum-tax rate, with SDFI + Equinor dividends noted as additional capture channels — do not present 78% as the fraction of total net wealth.
