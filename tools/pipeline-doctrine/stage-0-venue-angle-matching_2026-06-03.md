# Stage 0: venue-angle / register matching

**The catch (author, 2026-06-03).** A venue can be a perfect *topic/lane* fit and still be an *angle/register* mismatch. The $100 Barrel essay is squarely in Phenomenal World's lane (oil, pricing-critique, extraction political economy — PW runs all of it), but PW's *current editorial stance* is explicitly left, power- and sovereignty-focused, and anti-technocratic, while the essay's Condition-1 tuning makes it non-partisan / "honest accounting" / "architecture, not a program." Same topic, divergent register. That mismatch should have been caught at Stage 0, not noticed by the author reading the venue's site after the essay was done.

## The refinement
Stage-0 venue research must capture three layers, not one:
1. **Editorial brain + format + length** (already in the venue-norms doc) — what the venue rewards structurally.
2. **Current editorial STANCE / active angle** — the venue's *live* position on the essay's topic: recent-issue hot-angles, named initiatives (e.g., PW's "Financing the Energy Transition," 2024), the register of recent pieces (technocratic vs power-focused; non-partisan vs committed-left/right). Read the venue's *current* output, not just its masthead.
3. **Alignment check** — does the essay's angle/register *align with, fill a gap in, or clash with* the venue's current stance? Output one of: ALIGNED (submit as-is) / GAP-FILLING (submit; lead the pitch with the gap) / CLASH-BUT-SUBMITTABLE (re-angle the **pitch** to the venue's register; keep the essay's own integrity) / WRONG-VENUE (re-route).

## Two levers when there's a clash (cheapest first)
- **Re-angle the pitch, not the essay.** The pitch is the first contact; frame the essay's genuinely-present, venue-resonant dimensions (for $100B→PW: cost severance as a *power* relation; the Niger Delta extraction story; "the discount-rate debate prices the wrong thing," which rhymes with the venue's pricing-critique stream) and downplay the off-register notes (the center-right "architecture not a program" close). Honest framing only — don't promise a register the essay doesn't deliver.
- **Add a venue-resonant beat to the essay** — *only if it is framework-honest and additive on its own terms*, never as pandering. (See the renewables-cost-severance beat, 2026-06-03: it resonates with an energy-transition venue AND sharpens the residual-commons-value concept AND demonstrates the framework's neutrality — so it earns its place independent of the venue.)

## Discipline guardrail
Re-angling is framing + honest addition, never distortion. The essay's own Condition-1 / non-partisan integrity is not sacrificed to court a venue's lean — that would trade a durable property (non-tribal credibility, load-bearing for the book) for one submission. Match by *foregrounding* what is honestly there, not by becoming what the venue wants.
