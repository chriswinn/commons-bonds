## §1. Pass-3.4 scope reminder

Apply the **adversarial / detractor character set** to the full chapter. The adversarial set tests robustness: do hostile readers find threads that would let them unwind the artifact?

**The diagnostic is NOT pass/fail per character.** All adversarial characters return EXCLUDE — that is expected. The diagnostic is **which threads they collectively find**, and whether those threads are:

- (a) **Load-bearing chapter claims** — chapter must hold its ground; spot-fix would damage acceptance-test verdicts. Disposition: acknowledge the thread + verify the chapter's structural moves disarm it sufficiently + cross-chapter handoff if the thread warrants further engagement at another chapter.
- (b) **Procedural / cosmetic flags** — spot-fixes could disarm the attack without damaging acceptance verdicts. Disposition: apply spot-fixes.

**Adversarial verdict scale (different from acceptance):**
- **⚠ EXCLUDE** — would push back but the chapter holds against the push-back; thread is acknowledged.
- **⚠⚠ EXCLUDE** — would actively reject; finds load-bearing thread; chapter's structural moves don't disarm.
- **⚠⚠⚠ EXCLUDE** — would weaponize the chapter against itself; finds chapter-disqualifying thread.

NEUTRAL and INCLUDE verdicts are **not in scope** for adversarial characters — they are selected for hostile read; verdict-floor is EXCLUDE.

### §2.1 A1 — Reedville-side reader (industry-aligned; menhaden specifically) — ⚠⚠ EXCLUDE

**Read profile.** Works at or has family at Omega Protein / Ocean Harvesters in Reedville, VA (Northumberland County). Has heard the Bay-conservation arguments for decades and rejects them as urban-coastal politics dressed up as science. Reads the chapter for industry-villainization framing.

**What this character pulls:**

- **The value-disperses-cost-concentrates frame at L121** ("The value disperses across a continent and across industries. The cost concentrates here, on the rockfish, the ospreys, the watermen who depend on a Bay that depends on the menhaden.") — frames the reduction operation as a net-extractive industry, omits employment in Reedville (~280 jobs at Omega Protein), tax revenue to Northumberland County, the global protein-supply role of fish meal.
- **"Industrial reduction operation out of Reedville, Virginia" at L111** — singles out Reedville by name; the chapter's framing reads as targeting the specific industry employer.
- **The asymmetry framing at L83 + L115 + L119** ("committees acted with confidence" when offering increases vs "discover an unfamiliar caution" when offering reductions) — characterized as if the multi-state ASMFC compact is morally biased rather than reflecting legitimate stakeholder balancing.
- **O'Connor petition treated as central evidence at L123** — one VA resident's petition against an industry operating in compliance with ASMFC for two decades.

**What the chapter's structural moves disarm:**

- Chapter acknowledges "industry side regards as a hard-fought concession" at L115 — gives the industry posture institutional voice.
- Chapter does NOT name Omega Protein / Ocean Harvesters directly (per Stage 1 brief §8 hard constraint — held OUT per audience-load discipline for this reader specifically).
- Chapter routes the analytical critique through O'Connor's regulatory-record petition rather than the chapter narrator's voice — disarms the "this is just one author's opinion" pushback.
- Chapter's structural argument at L121 is about cost-severance pattern, not industry-villainization specifically; reader who reads carefully will see this.

**What the chapter does NOT disarm:**

- The chapter does not engage the employment / community-economy argument from Reedville's perspective. The Reedville-side reader's strongest pushback ("the reduction fishery is HOW Northumberland County eats") is not surfaced or addressed.
- This is **load-bearing (a)** for the chapter's argumentative surface — engaging the employment frame would compromise the chapter's central cost-severance argument.

**Verdict: ⚠⚠ EXCLUDE.** Active rejection; finds load-bearing thread; chapter's structural moves partially disarm.
