## M11 critic-survival probes against INCLUDE

### Probe A — "The defense engages four alternatives. Does the prose handle all four cleanly, or does one read as filler?"

Each rejection does specific structural work:
- *Externality* — names the cost-outside-transaction without naming the carrying-party.
- *Displacement* — names movement without naming the cut-tie.
- *Transfer* — names active routing without naming the missing routing-mechanism.
- *Appropriation* — moralizes the structural mechanism into actor-intent.

Four distinct failures, four distinct alternatives. None reads as filler.

### Probe B — "The employment-severance metaphor is labor-economics-coded. Is that a problem for the racial-economic dimension?"

Tier-B residual ⚠ flag for characters #8 and #15 (reparations specialist and Black readers). Mitigation: the Harvey lineage citation is explicit in the convergence; the Restitution Bond defense paragraph carries the direct racial-economic engagement at the bond-instrument level. The headline-mechanism paragraph's metaphor is defensible at the structural-mechanism level; the racial-economic deepening lands at the instruments.

### Probe C — "The closing line points at the bond instrument. Is that scope-creep?"

The closing line ("Naming the missing architecture is what makes the bond proposing it possible") closes the term-defense by naming what the choice enables — parallel to the bond-defense paragraph's closing ("The framework uses the term in continuity with these traditions"). The framework's apparatus is named in the closing only as the destination the term opens; the methodology + instrument material lives in later chapters. Scope-discipline holds.

### Probe D — "Mazzucato + Harvey convergence at this term-level might overlap with Ch 2 line 141–145's existing Mazzucato + Harvey citation."

The existing line 141–145 prose lineage-cites Mazzucato + Harvey for *value extraction* (the paired term). The proposed paragraph lineage-cites Mazzucato + Harvey for *cost severance* (the headline term) and frames the framework as the third sister-term that points at the missing payment-instrument the other two leave the reader to imagine. The two citations run as parallel disciplines, not redundancy — the framework is a deliberate three-step (value extraction → cost severance → bond posting) and each step gets its lineage acknowledgment.
