## 4. Group A — Framework tests

### 4.1 Test 1 — AIT Falsifiability Gate

**Scarcity axis:** specific non-substitutable attachments to specific people / kin / cultures / origins. Under abundance of such bonds — imagine every individual has infinite fully-substitutable attachment-relations with no identity-specific loss on severance — the cost evaporates. Not phenomenological residue; the structural harm (kindred severance) disappears the moment the scarcity (specific-non-substitutable attachment) disappears.

**Verdict:** PASS. Scarcity-grounded. This is the v1.21.0-ratified AIT verdict on the dimension (unchanged by the name choice; AIT operates on the cost-scarcity relationship per C9 §3, not the label).

### 4.2 Test 2 — Two-Path Rigor

**Path 1 — Allocation symmetry.**

- **Who captures value:** dominant-culture institutions (assimilation-requiring employers, legal / medical / state frameworks that naturalize one kinship form, majority-language school systems, extraction industries displacing indigenous communities). Also: individuals and firms whose access to substitutable labor, substitutable workforce, or substitutable community composition is the *point* of flattening specific attachments.
- **Who bears cost:** minorities, diaspora, indigenous communities, linguistic minorities, chosen-family-reliant populations (LGBTQ+, estranged-from-family, divorced-and-remarried, immigrant second-generation, adoptees, biological-family-rejected), and bearers of specific-identity-deep bonds whose substitution is forced or incentivized.
- **Structural?** Yes. The asymmetry derives from allocation architecture (assimilation-required access to work, legal recognition, public services). Not reducible to individual preference or transient market condition.
- **Tier assignment unambiguous?** Yes. Kindred sits squarely in the Tier 4 (Foreclosure) + Tier 5 (Non-Commodifiable) space per the eight-tier decomposition — specific bonds severed cannot be restored by substitution.

**Verdict (Path 1):** PASS.

**Path 2 — Shield absence.**

- **Shield type:** cultural distance (majority cannot see what it costs minorities) + *naturalization-of-dominant-kinship-form* (lay-English "family = blood" default that makes chosen-family bonds invisible in legal / medical / institutional accounting) + consent-normalization (when an assimilated individual's "choice" to accept dominant-culture norms is treated as terminally closing the accounting).
- **Structural?** Yes. Persists with full individual awareness. An assimilated second-generation immigrant fully aware of what language they are losing does not dissolve the shield — the shield is the majority-culture accounting framework that treats the loss as closed.
- **Correctly named?** Yes. The kindred-severance shield operates at the level of social-accounting terminus, parallel to the 120+ hour-week consent-normalization case in the Agency dimension (v1.2.1 §5.2 canonical treatment).
- **Does the architecture of the claim itself create a new shield?** No. Kindred names the dimension; the naming does not permit switch-arguing between dimensions because the merger/primitiveness boundaries are clean (see §4.3).

**Verdict (Path 2):** PASS.

**Combined verdict:** PASS two-path.

Bilateral independence holds. Merger gate PASS.

**Primitiveness gate.**

Does Kindred decompose into a composition of existing dimensions? Attempt:

- As Cohesion? No — civic-general trust (Cohesion) differs from identity-deep specific-bond (Kindred). Bilateral independence above demonstrates.
- As Institutional? No — institutional-hierarchy cost (Institutional) differs from identity-community cost (Kindred).
- As Informational? No — evidentiary-asymmetry differs from cultural-loss.
- As Agency? No — coercion-structure differs from attachment-non-substitutability.

Specific-non-substitutable-attachment-bond is its own scarcity axis. Primitiveness gate PASS.

**Combined verdict:** Both gates PASS. Kindred (as renamed dimension) remains distinct under v1.21.0 bilateral-independence constructions; no merger indicated; primitiveness preserved.

### 10.7 Methodological Defense sub-battery — M1 Analytic Philosopher

**M1.** "Is 'Kindred' doing real definitional work, or is it aesthetic?" Analysis: Kindred labels the dimension whose scarcity axis is *specific-non-substitutable attachment-bond*.
