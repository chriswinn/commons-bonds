## §2. Per-finding dispositions

### F-1 (HIGH; RULE-OF-THREE CLOSE LLM-CADENCE; SPOT-FIX APPLIED)

**Problem.** §I closing paragraph contained a classic LLM-cadence rule-of-three:

> *"The methodologies differ. The vocabularies differ. The political registers differ. And yet read together, the four books rhyme."*

Three short identically-structured declaratives ("The X differ.") in immediate succession before a contrast pivot ("And yet"). Per Stage 1 brief §2 ("Three-in-a-row declarative LLM cadence. Particularly dangerous in long-form review-essay; cumulative cadence over 7,000+ words flattens. Vary sentence length aggressively.") and "Rule-of-three closes. Vary rhythm at section closes; the rule-of-three is the load-bearing LLM-cadence-tell at this length."

**Recommendation ratified:** Collapse the triplet to a single sentence with internal parallel variation, preserving the contrast pivot to "And yet."

**Spot-fix applied:**
- **Before:** "The methodologies differ. The vocabularies differ. The political registers differ. And yet read together, the four books rhyme."
- **After:** "The methodologies differ, the vocabularies differ along with them, and so do the political registers. And yet read together, the four books rhyme."

The single-sentence revision preserves the differentiation-vs-rhyming contrast that anchors §I's pivot to the cumulative reading, while breaking the LLM-tic pattern and varying the rhythm before the "And yet" pivot.

Status: ✅ APPLIED 2026-05-27.

### F-2 (MED; CHRISTOPHERS RHETORICAL PAIRING; KEPT-AS-ARTISTIC-CHOICE)

**Problem.** §IV Christophers paragraph closes with: "*This is the mechanism of the renewable shortfall. The price is right. The profit is wrong.*"

Three short declaratives, technically a rule-of-three pattern. Triggered the discipline scan.

**Recommendation ratified:** Keep-as-artistic-choice. The "The price is right. The profit is wrong." pairing is a deliberate rhetorical mirror of Christophers's own central formulation ("profit, not price, is what reshapes the world"). The rhythm IS Christophers's register; the sentence-level cadence is part of the engagement with his analytical voice. The discipline scan's flag is structurally correct but contextually overridden by the artistic-choice consideration.

No spot-fix applied.

Status: 🔵 VERIFIED (kept-as-artistic-choice).

### F-3 (MED; CUMULATIVE-ANALYTICAL-PIVOT RHETORIC; KEPT-AS-ARTISTIC-CHOICE)

**Problem.** §VI cumulative-diagnosis paragraph contains: "*The architecture is engineered. It can be engineered differently. The four books name the location, the activity, the mechanism, and the metric.*"

Two short declaratives followed by an enumeration. Triggered the discipline scan.

**Recommendation ratified:** Keep-as-artistic-choice. The two-declarative pivot ("X is engineered. It can be engineered differently.") is the analytical pivot the cumulative-synthesis section turns on. The rhythm is deliberate; the enumeration that follows ("the location, the activity, the mechanism, and the metric") IS the four-book synthesis-spine. Tightening this would weaken the analytical claim.

No spot-fix applied.

Status: 🔵 VERIFIED (kept-as-artistic-choice).

### F-4 (MED; LLM-CADENCE OPENER; SPOT-FIX APPLIED)

**Problem.** §VII pre-coda paragraph opened with: *"It is also worth marking what such an architecture would not require by way of philosophical novelty. The intellectual foundations have been built."*

The "It is also worth marking..." opener is LLM-cadence-adjacent — meta-commentary register that diluted the substance-first directness NYRB literary-critical register prefers. Per Stage 1 brief §2 ban on meta-commentary openers ("What this means is..." / "It bears noting that...").

**Recommendation ratified:** Substance-first revision. Drop the meta-opener; lead with the claim.

**Spot-fix applied:**
- **Before:** "It is also worth marking what such an architecture would not require by way of philosophical novelty. The intellectual foundations have been built."
- **After:** "Such an architecture would not require new philosophical foundations. The intellectual foundations have been built."

The revision tightens the opener to substance, removes the meta-commentary register, and preserves the analytical move into the second sentence ("The intellectual foundations have been built.").

Status: ✅ APPLIED 2026-05-27.

### F-5 (LOW; PARALLEL-CADENCE DENSITY; SPOT-FIX APPLIED)

**Problem.** §VI four-scale-synthesis sentence contained four consecutive "at the level of X" constructions:

> *"A redirected national accounting at the level of growth, combined with a redesigned pricing mechanism at the level of the transaction, combined with a corrected diagnostic frame at the level of value, combined with an adjusted institutional location at the level of legal modules, is what..."*

Four identical structural-parallel patterns in a single sentence. Sentence-level tic-density that compounds the whole-essay use of the phrase. Heavy parallel-cadence reads as LLM-prose patterning even when each individual instance is analytically warranted.

**Recommendation ratified:** Vary the four instances; preserve one or two; convert the others to simpler positional phrasing.

**Spot-fix applied:**
- **Before:** "at the level of growth, combined with... at the level of the transaction, combined with... at the level of value, combined with... at the level of legal modules"
- **After:** "at the growth level, combined with... at the transaction level, [drop 'combined with'], a corrected diagnostic frame for value, and an adjusted institutional location at the legal-modules level"

The revision: 4 × "at the level of X" → 1 × "at the level of" (none retained) + 3 simpler positional forms ("at the growth level"; "for value"; "at the legal-modules level"). Whole-essay "at the level of" count: 9 line-matches → 8 line-matches (the single highly-dense line tightened; remaining instances spread across other paragraphs at acceptable density).

Status: ✅ APPLIED 2026-05-27.
