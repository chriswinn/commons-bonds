## Summary

Two MUST-FIX findings (both definitional/proof-logic at the framework's central equation); eight SHOULD-FIX findings (mix of cross-reference targets, internal-consistency, numerical-labeling, and proof-bridge tightness); seven LOW findings (cosmetic numbering and notation, punted to Pass 2 typography sweep).

The Tech Appendix's mathematical apparatus is largely sound. Theorems 10.1a, 10.3, 10.4 (proofs of both sufficient-condition cases + knife-edge corollary), and the §17 sum-of-costs generalization are correctly derived. The Norway Method-3 numerical worked example (§11.5) computes correctly. The four-gates apparatus (§7) is internally coherent.

The two MUST-FIX issues both sit at the framework's structural identity — the central equation **CS = RCV − B** — and trace to a single root cause: §5's introduction of the two-instrument decomposition `total CS = (CSD − B₁) + (RCV − B₂)` was added to the framework without back-propagating the implications to §1.7 (Definition 1.7), §10.1a (Theorem 10.1a Assumption A1), §17.5 (CS preservation claim), and §11.5/§11.6 (empirical worked-cases using `CS = RCV − B`). Cleanly fixing this requires either explicit nomenclature (e.g., `CS_forward` vs. `CS_total`) or a clarifying note that anchors `CS = RCV − B` as a forward-looking-only special case of the §5 decomposition.

Cross-references from Ch 6 prose to Tech Appendix anchors (#sec-7-four-gates, #sec-8-asymmetric-regret-rule, #sec-17-formula-generalization) verified accurate.
