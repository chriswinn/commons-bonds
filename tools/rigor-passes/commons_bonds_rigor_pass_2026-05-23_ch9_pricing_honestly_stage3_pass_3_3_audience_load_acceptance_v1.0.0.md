## §1. Pass-3.3 scope reminder

Pass 3.3 applies the **acceptance-test character set** to the full chapter. The acceptance set is the target-audience set: characters whose No vote kills the chapter (Tier 1 gating) + whose Yes vote amplifies the chapter (Tier 2 pipeline-strengthening) + whose experience the chapter engages (Tier 3 cultural-resonance / accessibility / practitioner). 18 characters audited here (3 Tier 1, 4 Tier 2, 11 Tier 3); supplementary characters flagged for optional next-session expansion.

**Verdict scale** (per template):
- **✓✓✓ INCLUDE** — strong yes; chapter lands decisively for this audience.
- **✓✓ INCLUDE** — yes; chapter lands.
- **✓ INCLUDE** — yes with minor reservations.
- **NEUTRAL** — neither alienated nor strongly engaged.
- **⚠ EXCLUDE** — may disengage; flag specific reason.
- **⚠⚠ EXCLUDE** — actively rejects; flag specific reason.
- **⚠⚠⚠ EXCLUDE** — disqualifying for this audience; cannot ship to venue with this character as Tier 1.

**Per-character format:** Read-for / Lands / Flags / Through-the-close / Verdict.

**Specific Reading-C-v3-related questions to track per character** (Pass 2 §11 carry-forward):
- Does the asymmetric framework-Public-Choice framing (Public Choice = extractor's reasoning under incomplete cost-visibility; framework = the complete ledger) land for this character?
