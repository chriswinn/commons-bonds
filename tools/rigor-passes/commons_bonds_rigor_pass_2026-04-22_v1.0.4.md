## 4. Spatial — confirmation pass (Group A–F + remaining 22 characters)

### 4.1 Group A — framework tests

**AIT:** Scarcity stripped = distance/place-specificity. Under abundance (frictionless relocation, no stranded-asset cost), place-specific bargaining asymmetry evaporates. **PASS.**

**Two-path:** Path 1 — firms capture locational advantage; stranded populations / low-bargaining-power regions bear cost. Path 2 — distance itself is the shield; place-specificity makes exit costly; shield is structural per v1.21.0. **PASS both paths.**

**Merger gate under "Spatial":**
- vs. Habitability: shared-medium (air / climate substrate) ≠ place / distance. Bilateral independence holds. PASS.
- vs. Ecosystem: services-from-biosphere ≠ place. PASS.
- vs. Kindred: identity-deep bond to specific place is Kindred territory (place-attachment overlap). Spatial prices the *economic bargaining cost of immobility*, not the identity-specific loss of place. Bilateral independence: Appalachian community with rich kindred-bonds to place AND high geographic-immobility cost activates both. But cases exist where Spatial activates without Kindred (low-kindred migrant worker stranded by H-2A visa place-binding) and where Kindred activates without Spatial (diasporic community fully-relocated but still bearing identity-specific displacement cost). PASS.
- vs. Institutional: hierarchy ≠ place. PASS.

**Primitiveness:** Location-as-scarcity is primitive. PASS.

**Multi-scale × 2 env:** *This is the Spatial rename's raison d'être.* Individual (Earth worker bound by employer-sponsored visa ↔ off-Earth crew member on long-duration asteroid-mining contract where return is logistically foreclosed); Community (extractive sacrifice zone ↔ orbital-habitat community with crew drawn from specific Earth origins); Government (housing-policy jurisdiction ↔ Outer Space Treaty signatory regulating off-Earth-site licensing); Corporate (employer-region lock-in ↔ off-Earth-logistics corp pricing crew-origin-continuity costs). **All four scales × both environments PASS.** The rename specifically *resolves* the off-Earth multi-scale strain documented in v1.0.0 stress test.

Institutional (§2.4) prices organizational hierarchy — Autonomy can operate without Institutional (gig worker coerced by algorithm, not hierarchy). Temporal (§2.3) prices time-separation — 120-hour-week activates both Autonomy (coerced) and Temporal (deferred-health-cost); the dimensions price different aspects of the same case. Epistemic (§2.9) prices evidentiary precondition of consent (Kantian pair). Kindred (§2.5) prices identity-deep specific-attachment — cultural-assimilation case activates both (Autonomy coercion + Kindred-specific-attachment-severance). Strip Autonomy alone: hierarchy, time-structure, epistemic-access, and specific-attachment-recognition all remain, but coerced-choice-as-shield collapses and workers can refuse the terms that were accepted under compulsion.

### 10.4 Cohesion (retained)

**Summary.** Cohesion names the civic-trust / collective-action-infrastructure / non-kin-coordination-capacity dimension: capacity for public life, stranger-coordination, civic-institution participation, commons-governance, informal mutual aid, democratic participation-as-substrate. The dimension prices the structural erosion of collective-coordination capacity by market-expansion, employer-power, platform-atomization, surveillance-anxiety, and trust-destruction. "Cohesion" (retained) engages Putnam's social-capital literature and Ostrom's commons-governance literature as intellectual ancestors; the framework extends them with tier-decomposable pricing.

**What becomes visible under AIT inversion.** Under civic-trust abundance (dense non-kin coordination at zero friction; robust civic infrastructure; informal social control functioning; public-life-as-substrate intact), gig workers form effective non-employer associations; communities successfully govern commons (Ostrom's 8 design principles at work); neighborhoods maintain informal social control; mutual aid is the default safety net; public goods are over-provisioned through voluntary coordination; democracy runs on calibrated trust; stranger-to-stranger interaction is low-friction. *The NYC knowledge worker gains the capacity to rely on neighbors, participate in block-level governance, and engage substantively in local democracy — without changing any of her Kindred bonds.*

**Case cluster.** Bowling Alone suburbs (Putnam's canonical case); gig-economy atomization; NYC consulting-years isolation (canonical non-co-occurring with Kindred per v1.21.0 ratification — demonstrates Cohesion scarcity at Kindred abundance); knowledge-worker atomization at urban concentration; social-capital erosion via surveillance capitalism; platform-mediation replacing civic trust (Airbnb / Uber / DoorDash displacing neighbor-stay / community-carpool / diner-hospitality); commons-governance failures where communities couldn't coordinate resistance; civic-institution defunding (libraries, community centers, schools, local newspapers); Ostrom-successor commons failure cases.

**Other relevant observations.**
- **Putnam-overlap is a feature, not a bug.** Social-capital literature is the scholarly ancestor; framework extends it with tier-decomposable pricing Putnam didn't have. Methodology-appendix engagement turns the "borrowed from Putnam" concern into a strength.
- **Ostrom-successor reader (Character 17) alignment is strongest of any candidate** — commons-governance extended into cost-severance pricing is exactly what this reader is looking for; the dimension name Cohesion signals the scholarly lineage directly.
- **The canonical non-co-occurring case (NYC consulting-years isolation) is pedagogically load-bearing** — introducing Cohesion in contrast to Kindred is the chapter-level teaching technique that makes the distinction real to readers.
- **Candidate alternatives rejected** — Community (Kindred-overlap), Culture (stronger Kindred-overlap), Social-Fabric (hyphenation + metaphor), Civic (lateral move, breaks abstract-noun pattern), narrower alternatives (Civic-Ties, Communion, Fellowship, Solidarity, Conviviality) dominated.
- **Pre-drafting note:** methodology-appendix Putnam-engagement prose; dimension-intro positioning statement distinguishing from Kindred; NYC canonical non-co-occurring case must appear in Cohesion's dimension-intro per v1.21.0 pedagogical requirement.

**Relevant literature.**
- *Social capital:* Putnam, *Bowling Alone* (2000); Coleman, *Foundations of Social Theory* (1990); Bourdieu, "The Forms of Capital" (1986).
- *Commons governance:* Ostrom, *Governing the Commons* (1990) — commons require civic trust to function; Hess & Ostrom, *Understanding Knowledge as a Commons* (2007).
- *Gemeinschaft / Gesellschaft distinction:* Tönnies, *Gemeinschaft und Gesellschaft* (1887) — the Kindred / Cohesion split maps to Tönnies's distinction.
- *Civic life / public space:* Jacobs, *Death and Life of Great American Cities* (1961) — eyes-on-the-street as civic-coordination infrastructure; Sennett, *The Fall of Public Man* (1977).
- *Loneliness / atomization:* Murthy, *Together: The Healing Power of Human Connection in a Sometimes Lonely World* (2020); Klinenberg, *Palaces for the People* (2018) — social infrastructure as civic-cohesion scaffolding.
- *Platform atomization:* Rosenblat, *Uberland* (2018); Gray & Suri, *Ghost Work* (2019).

**What's uniquely priced vs. adjacent dimensions.** Cohesion prices civic-general non-kin collective-coordination capacity. Kindred (§2.5) prices specific identity-deep bonds — the NYC consulting case is the canonical non-co-occurring demonstration (Kindred abundance, Cohesion scarcity). Institutional (§2.4) prices organizational hierarchy — Cohesion is civic-trust-between-strangers, not workplace-coordination. Political (§2.7) prices distributional-conflict capture — civic cohesion is the substrate that makes political contestation possible, but Cohesion prices the substrate itself, not the distributional conflict. Strip Cohesion alone: family / friend / workplace coordination intact (Kindred / Institutional), but the neighborhood-as-infrastructure reappears — gaining the capacity to act collectively with strangers in ways the NYC knowledge worker currently cannot.
