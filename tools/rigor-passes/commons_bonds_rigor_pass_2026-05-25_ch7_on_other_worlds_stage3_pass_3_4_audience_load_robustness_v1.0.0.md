### §2.2 A2 — Off-world-extraction-ethics-skeptic (deep-ecology) — ⚠ EXCLUDE (ABSORBED)

**Threads pulled.**

- **"Extract freely" cluster across multiple scenarios.** Asteroid iron at line 85 (*"Extract freely. Under hostile read, the cumulative extract-freely cluster reads as instrumental-economic-framing of celestial bodies that the deep-ecology tradition rejects in principle.
- **"Aggressively" word choice at line 91.** Under hostile read, *"extract aggressively"* is extractive-coded vocabulary; the surrounding asymmetric-regret-flip framing does not neutralize the word choice for a reader who reads "aggressively" as carrying ethical weight.
- **Instrumental-pricing-framework throughout.** Chapter operates entirely within a cost-benefit-analytical register; intrinsic-value-of-nature framings are nowhere endorsed in the chapter. Adversarial reader reads as the framework's ontology being instrumental-only.

**Chapter's structural moves that disarm.**

- **Europa-boundary case at line 93.** *"The scientific and moral value of potentially destroying an independent biosphere... is genuinely not priceable in dollars. **Net read.** Strict deep-ecology adversarial reader pulls hard on the cumulative extract-freely framing + the instrumental-pricing-framework default. Chapter's structural moves (Europa-boundary methodological humility; explicit non-priceable-values acknowledgment in Mars-water case; parameter-sensitivity discipline; Mars rare-earths self-bearing externality) provide substantive disarm at the close-read level. The deeper philosophical disagreement (instrumental-vs-intrinsic-value-of-nature) is a tradition-level disagreement the chapter is not designed to resolve — the chapter operates in commons-pricing tradition, not in environmental-ethics-tradition. **⚠ EXCLUDE: would push back at the deeper-philosophical-disagreement level; chapter's structural moves engage at the methodological-honesty level; thread is acknowledged in pre-pub review queue.** Not chapter-disqualifying; this is normal scholarly-disagreement-within-tradition framing.
