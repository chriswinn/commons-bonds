## §1. Why this discipline exists

### §1.1 The empirical artifact (Noema V-D, 2026-05-28)

The Noema essay Version D (hybrid) audit history surfaced a methodological
finding that the rest of the Wave 2 portfolio has not yet absorbed: **three
independent audits (drafter's-self-audit + prior-independent + fresh-second-
independent) converged on findings that any two-audit subset would have
missed.**

The specific anchors:

| Finding | Drafter | Prior-independent | Fresh-second-independent | 2-audit verdict | 3-audit verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **T3.11 Chesapeake regional downgrade ✓✓✓ → ✓✓** | wash (held) | DOWNGRADE | DOWNGRADE | drafter alone vs prior-independent = 1-vs-1; ambiguous | Two independents agree → strong signal; SF-VD-1 anchored |
| **SF-VD-1 spot-fix (Ostrom repositioning after title-earning sentence)** | not surfaced | SURFACED | SURFACED via Pass 3.5 (F-DE-VD-H1) lens | Surfaced by one; might be over-flagged | Independent convergence across two independent reads + cross-lens validation |
| **F-DE-VD-H1 HIGH (§VI cadence-arc broken)** | not surfaced | not surfaced (Pass 3.5 was missing — "inherits clean" asserted without fresh test) | SURFACED at HIGH severity | Would have shipped without flagging | Caught the gap |
| **F-DE-VD-M1 MEDIUM (§Close lineage-citation asymmetry)** | partial acknowledgment (§8.1 #3 framed as fair-use prudence) | not surfaced | SURFACED at MEDIUM | Would have been under-credited | Surfaced + framed as developmental-edit equal-weighting concern |
| **T9 + T10 NEW adversarial threads at A8** | not surfaced | not surfaced | SURFACED + drove A8 ⚠ → ⚠⚠ downgrade | Would have shipped at ⚠ | Caught + flagged for Stage 5 pre-pub-queue |

### §1.2 The methodological claim

**Three-audit triangulation captures findings that two-audit triangulation
misses, and the marginal cost is bandwidth-cheap under the no-cost-pressure
framing.**

Under the 2026-05-28 ratified
[`feedback_token_cost_not_a_constraint.md`](../memory/feedback_token_cost_not_a_constraint.md),
the triangulation should become **the default discipline for every Wave 2
derivative essay** rather than an artifact of opportunity that fires only when
the author notices a chip got under-specified (the empirical anchor — the
Noema V-D second-independent audit was commissioned precisely because the
prior chip omitted Pass 3.5).

### §1.3 Why two-audit triangulation is insufficient

The drafter has a structural bias: the drafter expects the draft to land well
(otherwise the draft would have been revised before audit). Even with
methodological caveats (the drafter's-self-audit explicitly flags "bias
surface area"), the drafter's verdicts cluster at "wash + marginal uplift"
patterns. The drafter's-self-audit on the Noema V-D scored 7 ✓✓✓ + 5 ✓✓ + 2 ✓
at Pass 3.3 — the most-positive of the three reads.

A single prior-independent audit removes the drafter-bias but introduces
single-auditor-lens-bias: any one independent audit has a particular reading
the auditor brings to the prose. The prior-independent on Noema V-D missed
the T1.1 downgrade + the A8 downgrade + (load-bearing) ran no fresh Pass 3.5,
asserting "inherits clean" without testing.

Three audits — drafter + two independents — produce a convergence/divergence
table that surfaces:
- **Three-way agreement** → highest-confidence findings (ratify as recommended)
- **Two-independent vs drafter** → second-highest-confidence finding (the
  independents converged against drafter-bias; ratify in the independents'
  direction)
- **One independent + drafter vs second independent** → calibration question;
  reasonable readings differ; author judgment on the call
- **Three-way divergence** → genuine ambiguity; surface directly to author

Noema V-D produced two of these patterns (T3.11 = 2-independent-vs-drafter;
T1.1 = 1-independent-and-drafter-vs-second-independent) AND a YOU-only Pass
3.5 finding (F-DE-VD-M1 MEDIUM). Both patterns are diagnostically informative
in different ways. Under two-audit methodology, neither pattern would have
been visible — there is no "two of three" in a two-audit world.

## §2. Methodology specification

### §2.1 The three audits (canonical names + scope)

#### §2.1.1 Drafter's-self-audit

- **Produced by:** the Stage 2 audience-blind drafting session, at draft
  completion, in the same session that produced the V-D (hybrid) prose.
- **Methodological status:** explicitly flagged as drafter-bias-susceptible;
  NOT authoritative as a rigor pass; documents the drafter's expected
  Pass 3.3 + 3.4 + 3.5 + Stage 4 + Stage 5 verdicts so the independent
  sessions have a concrete cross-check target.
- **Scope:** per-character expected verdicts at Pass 3.3 (acceptance scale
  ✓✓✓ → ✓✓ → ✓ → ⚠ → ⚠⚠ → ⚠⚠⚠); thread-pull synthesis expected at Pass 3.4;
  per-finding expected restorations at Pass 3.5.
- **Format:** per Aeon V-D + Noema V-D drafter's-self-audit precedents at
  `_archive/parallel-drafts_<DATE>/<essay>-essay_hybrid_drafters-self-audit.md`.
- **Required §0 caveat:** explicit drafter-bias disclosure + named surfaces
  where drafter-bias is most active (e.g., "this draft built believing
  the citation augmentation would uplift characters #5, #7, #9, #2" — the
  drafter is reproducing those uplift claims here; whether they materialize
  at fresh-session scoring is exactly what the independent audits should
  test).

#### §2.1.2 Prior-independent audit (audit #1)

- **Produced by:** a fresh CC session spawned with explicit "do NOT read
  drafter's-self-audit until Phase 3 cross-check" instruction.
- **Methodological status:** the first independent rigor verdict; covers the
  full pass scope per v3.1 explicit-gate cascade (Pass 3.3 + 3.4 + 3.5 if
  whole-essay scale; Stage 4 + Stage 5 if pre-pub gate).
- **Scope:** ALL pass-3-3 acceptance characters + ALL pass-3-4 adversarial
  characters + a FRESH Pass 3.5 developmental-edit lens (NOT "inherits clean"
  by assertion).
- **Independence boundary:** Phase 2 scoring (the rigor verdicts) is locked
  + filed in the artifact BEFORE Phase 3 reads the drafter's-self-audit.
- **Format:** per Noema V-D prior-independent precedent at
  `publishing/essays/<essay>/rigor/pass-3-3-and-3-4-bundled-audience-load_VERSION-D_INDEPENDENT-AUDIT_<DATE>.md`
  (file name convention preserves backwards-compatibility for essays where the
  prior-independent audit already exists).

#### §2.1.3 Fresh-second-independent audit (audit #2)

- **Produced by:** a fresh CC session spawned with explicit "do NOT read
  drafter's-self-audit OR prior-independent audit until Phase 3 cross-check"
  instruction.
- **Methodological status:** the triangulating audit; the value-add is
  independence from BOTH drafter AND prior-independent.
- **Scope:** ALL pass-3-3 + ALL pass-3-4 + FRESH Pass 3.5; cross-check
  three-way at Phase 3.
- **Independence boundary:** Phase 2 scoring is locked + filed in the
  artifact BEFORE Phase 3 reads either prior artifact.
- **Format:** per Noema V-D fresh-second-independent precedent at
  `publishing/essays/<essay>/rigor/pass-3-3-and-3-4-and-3-5-bundled_VERSION-D_SECOND-INDEPENDENT-AUDIT_<DATE>.md`
  (note: the naming convention includes "-AND-3-5-" to signal the Pass 3.5
  fill-in role explicitly when applicable).

### §2.2 When the triangulation fires (default + exceptions)

**Default:** every Wave 2 derivative essay AT Stage 2 drafted-prose completion
+ drafter's-self-audit filing fires both independent audits IN PARALLEL
(run_in_background) since their independence is preserved by NOT reading each
other's outputs until Phase 3.

**Exceptions (triangulation does NOT fire):**

1. **Essays already SUBMITTED** — retroactive triangulation is moot; the
   prose is at the venue.
2. **Essays at Stage 1 brief without drafted prose** — triangulation requires
   prose to audit.
3. **Essays where author explicitly declines triangulation** — override
   preserved (e.g., a deliberate ship-as-is decision under submission-window
   pressure).
4. **Short-form (op-eds; pitches under ~500w)** — per
   `feedback_audience_aware_drafting_discipline.md` v2.0 Amendment C
   domain-of-applicability, short-form with strong iterated control is a
   regime where the discipline may not pay back its bandwidth cost. Aeon
   pitch (~289w V-E) historically used a single independent audit; the
   short-form-with-iterated-control exception is preserved here. **Note:**
   long-form op-eds (~900w) sit at the boundary; default to triangulation
   for op-eds derivative of book chapters (Norway, McDowell) given their
   audience-load complexity matches essay-scope.

**Trigger:** the moment the V-D (or analogous hybridized) draft + drafter's-
self-audit lands as a parallel-drafts archive artifact. The PM session (or
the drafting session itself) spawns both independent audit chips in the same
parallel-session-hop batch.

### §2.3 Cross-check convergence interpretation

Per the Noema V-D empirical anchor:

- **3 audits converge** → strongest signal; ratify as recommended; close the
  finding at the converged disposition.
- **2 audits converge against drafter** → strong signal in the converged
  direction; author judgment on whether to follow. **Default disposition:
  follow the independents.** Drafter-bias is structural; two independents
  agreeing against drafter is the discipline's strongest single signal.
- **2 audits converge against second-independent** → calibration question;
  reasonable readings differ; author surfaces directly + makes the call.
  Document the divergence explicitly in the audit's §12 cross-check table.
- **All 3 diverge** → genuine ambiguity; author surfaces directly + makes
  the call. Document the three positions + each audit's reasoning.

The Noema V-D empirical anchor for the **2-audit-misses-the-finding** failure
mode: T3.11 — drafter said wash; both independents said downgrade. The
2-vs-1 convergence drove SF-VD-1. Under 2-audit-only methodology (drafter +
prior-independent), this finding would have been one-against-one, and the
author would have had less confidence in which direction to go. Under the
triangulation, the 2-of-3 convergence is the deciding signal.

### §2.4 Cost-benefit framing

#### §2.4.1 Marginal token cost

- Drafter's-self-audit: ~10-20K tokens (the drafter is already in-session;
  the self-audit is a small marginal artifact).
- Prior-independent audit: ~50-150K tokens per audit (depending on essay
  scale + pass scope; Aeon V-D was ~50K; Noema V-D was ~120K; Harper's V-D V2
  was ~180K for the Pass 3.5 fill-in + hard-rule axis).
- Fresh-second-independent audit: ~50-150K tokens per audit.
- **Total marginal cost vs. prior 2-audit pattern: ~100-300K tokens per
  essay.**

Under no-cost-pressure framing per `feedback_token_cost_not_a_constraint.md`,
this is bandwidth-cheap. Do not cite tokens as the cost in cost-benefit
calculations.

#### §2.4.2 Marginal author-attention cost

- **Zero or near-zero.** The author reads the converged finding, not the
  three independent verdict tables. The triangulation produces compressed
  signal at author ratification time.
- The author's read at ratification is the §12 cross-check table (3-7
  divergence points typically; everything else is convergence) + the
  recommendations §. The full per-pass per-character tables are reference
  artifacts; the author reads them only if surfacing-bigger-issue mode
  fires.

#### §2.4.3 Marginal clock-time cost

- ~30-60 min per essay if independents fire in parallel (run_in_background).
- Zero if essays are not on a tight submission window — the parallel firing
  + Phase 3 cross-check at boundary is fully asynchronous.

#### §2.4.4 The cost that DOES bind

Per `feedback_token_cost_not_a_constraint.md`:
- **Author attention.** The §12 cross-check read + ratification disposition
  is the binding cost. For a Wave 2 portfolio of 4 essays, that's ~4 × ~10-15
  min = ~1 hour of author cognitive load. Compare to the alternative cost of
  the discipline NOT firing: a Noema-V-D-class missed finding shipped to
  publisher; downstream embarrassment + revision cycle.

- **Cumulative pipeline clock-time.** If the triangulation defers an essay
  submission by ~24-48 hours (the parallel-session fire + Phase 3 cross-check
  + ratification disposition), the trade-off is "ship 1 day later" vs. "ship
  with potential missed finding." For Wave 2 essays at Q4 2026 / Q1 2027
  submission windows, this is no trade-off — ample slack.

### §2.5 Discipline-cost framing (what the discipline buys + what it costs)

**Without triangulation (2-audit baseline):**
- False-positive risk: drafter-biased verdicts ratified as truth (e.g.,
  drafter's "wash" on T3.11 would have been ratified).
- False-negative risk: genuine findings missed because single auditor's
  lens didn't catch them (e.g., F-DE-VD-M1 lineage-citation asymmetry).
- Pass 3.5 omission risk: if the prior-independent's chip is under-specified
  (Pass 3.5 omitted), the gap is invisible; the prior-independent asserts
  "inherits clean" + ships. (The Noema V-D + Foreign Affairs V-D + NYRB V-D
  + Harper's V-D all hit this pattern; the second-independent V2 audits
  exist precisely because the original chip omitted Pass 3.5.)

**With triangulation:**
- Convergence signal is stronger (3-way ratifications are decisive).
- Divergences are explicit + addressable (the §12 cross-check table makes
  them visible).
- Pass 3.5 omission is structurally prevented (the fresh-second-independent's
  scope ALWAYS includes Pass 3.5 fresh).
- The discipline-cost is small relative to the expected value of catching
  one Noema-V-D-class missed finding per essay.

**The Aeon V-D + Noema V-D empirical anchors** demonstrate triangulation
surfacing material findings that 2-audit methodology missed:
- **Aeon V-D:** ✓✓ → ✓✓✓ upgrades the drafter over-credited at 3 characters
  (independent audits caught the over-crediting + recalibrated).
- **Noema V-D:** F-DE-VD-H1 HIGH + F-DE-VD-M1 MEDIUM that prior-independent
  did not surface (the second-independent's Pass 3.5 fill-in caught them).

## §6. Memory-entry decision

### §6.1 Should this become a memory entry?

**Recommendation: YES.** Codify as `tools/memory/feedback_portfolio_
triangulation_discipline.md` (draft in §7 of this handoff).

1. **The discipline is the default, not the exception.** Under no-cost-
   pressure framing per `feedback_token_cost_not_a_constraint.md`, the
   triangulation should fire on every Wave 2 derivative essay. Memory-entry
   codification is the right home for cross-session disciplines.

   trigger the drafter's-self-audit + two independents unless the discipline
   is in the memory layer. CLAUDE.md `@import` chain currently loads three
   always-load memory files; this should be a fourth (or a v3.1 extension
   memory pointer).

   and-3-5-bundled_VERSION-D_SECOND-INDEPENDENT-AUDIT_2026-05-28.md` §12.

   The prior-independent's "inherits clean" Pass 3.5 omission pattern
   repeated across Aeon + Harper's + FA + NYRB V-D audits; only the V2
