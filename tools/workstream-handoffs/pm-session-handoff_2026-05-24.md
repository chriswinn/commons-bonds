**Reasoning (four points):**

**(a) Response windows are 4–8 weeks; rapid submission KEEPS the pipeline fuller, not emptier.** Aeon ~8wk; Noema 4–6wk; BR 6–8wk; PW variable; Atlantic 4–6wk. **(b) Different essays at different venues are NOT simultaneous-submissions.** No publisher-side conflict. Simultaneous-submission policies prohibit the same piece to multiple venues at once; they don't constrain different pieces in parallel.

**(c) Per decisions-log 2026-05-08, the agent-pitch platform-paragraph floor is "≥1 essay at editor-review stage at a top-tier venue."** Four essays under review is well above this floor and reads as legitimately *active*, not as drawn-out. Holding ready essays in the drawer when the platform-criterion is already met would read as either over-engineering or risk-aversion to a literary agent — neither helps the pitch.

**(d) Rate-limiting factor is YOUR ratification bandwidth, not external constraint.** Per June schedule §"Rate-limiting factors," ~2 interactive ratifications/day sustainably. Five ratifications across 2–3 days clears the gate; the remaining is portal-submission time (~20 min each).
