4. Strategic call — submit more than 2 in parallel?

Recommendation
YES — submit all four essays that are ready (or near-ready) within the next 7 days. Do NOT artificially delay.

Reasoning

  • Response windows are 4–8 weeks; rapid submission KEEPS the pipeline fuller, not emptier. Aeon ~8wk · Noema 4–6wk · BR 6–8wk · PW variable · Atlantic 4–6wk. If you submit Mon May 25 – Sat May 31 (4 essays): first acknowledgments early June; first decisions late June–late July; last decisions mid-August. Through Wave 1 agent-query window (late July / early August), 3–4 essays are still under review. Spacing out ~1/week would NOT meaningfully extend "under review" status — the long tail of response windows already covers Wave 1.
  • Different essays at different venues are NOT simultaneous-submissions. Simultaneous-submission policies prohibit the same piece to multiple venues; they don't constrain different pieces in parallel.
  • The agent-pitch platform-paragraph floor is "≥1 essay at editor-review at a top-tier venue" per decisions-log 2026-05-08. Four essays under review is well above floor and reads as legitimately active, not as drawn-out. Holding ready essays in the drawer when the criterion is already met would read as risk-aversion or over-engineering — neither helps the pitch.
  • Rate-limiting factor is YOUR ratification bandwidth, not external constraint. ~2 interactive ratifications/day sustainably; 5 across 2–3 days clears the gate.
