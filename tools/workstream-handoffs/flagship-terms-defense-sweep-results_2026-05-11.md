## Per-term gap audit — verdict and reasoning

### Term 1 — Cost Severance (CS): GAP CONFIRMED

**Existing body-prose treatment:**

- **Ch 2 line 125:** "Economists call this an externality. The word is technically accurate and practically insufficient. An externality is a cost that falls outside the transaction. What happened in McDowell County is that the costs fell on specific people, in specific bodies, in specific communities, and those people are still paying them." — Brief alternative-rejection (against *externality*).
- **Ch 2 lines 131–139:** Vocabulary-section introduction with definition: "*Cost severance* is the mechanism by which costs are cut loose from the transactions that create them and transferred to parties who did not participate in the value extraction..." — Mechanism-defining work; pure definitional.
- **Ch 2 lines 141–145:** Lineage citation for the paired term *value extraction* (Mazzucato 2018, Harvey 2003). — Lineage work for paired term, NOT for severance itself.
- **Glossary lines 149–150:** Employment-severance metaphor argument (severance pay discharges a residual obligation; cost severance is the same logic applied to commons extraction). — Internal-scaffolding only; not body prose.

**What is missing:**

- Multi-alternative structural rejection (severance vs. externality / displacement / transfer / appropriation), parallel to the bond-defense's tax/fee/fund rejection. Only *externality* is engaged, and only briefly + rhetorically rather than structurally.
- Why-severance-specifically — the employment-severance metaphor that surfaces a missing payment-architecture lives in the glossary, not in body prose.
- Structural-feature argument: severance carries a borrowed payment-architecture (severance pay → bond posting); the alternatives don't carry an instrumented architecture by analogy. Body prose has not made this argument.

**Audience-load pressure-test impact (20-character set):** 16 INCLUDE / 4 NEUTRAL / 0 EXCLUDE. Four ✓✓✓ at the gating audiences (literary agent, Mazzucato/Raworth reader, welfare-economics reviewer, Coasean skeptic) — same audiences the bond-defense paragraph closes for at the instrument level. The headline-mechanism term is the framework's most-load-bearing umbrella choice; defending it body-prose closes the same kind of pitch + comp-cluster + M6-defusing gap.
