## BOTTOM LINE — the defensible surviving formulation

> Ostrom's design principles govern common-pool resources held by a **bounded, repeatedly-
> interacting set of HETEROGENEOUS appropriators who substantially share the resource's fate**
> (the value-capturers are largely the cost-bearers); her own foundational groundwater work
> (1965, West Basin) sits inside this shared-fate domain. A separate tradition — **Kapp's
> social-cost / cost-shifting analysis (1950), political ecology / environmental justice
> (Martínez-Alier, Bullard), and the power critique of the commons framework (Mudliar &
> Koontz 2021; Harvey)** — diagnosed the extraction asymmetry (a powerful value-capturer shifts
> cost onto an absent, diffuse, or dominated cost-bearer). The **Polanyi/Fraser** lineage
> diagnosed the fictitious-commodity / expropriation and **resists monetization on principle**
> (it treats pricing nature as the problem, not the cure). What none of these traditions — nor
> any adjacent valuation, accounting, bonding, or assembly (SEEA, ecosystem-services valuation,
> TEEB/True-Cost-Accounting, reclamation/assurance bonds, NRDA) — produced is a defensible
> **per-extraction-event residual-commons price with an anticipatory reserve posted against
> that specific figure.** The framework's contribution is that instrument and that integrated
> configuration, applied per event; it **prices, as honest cost-accounting, what the
> diagnostic traditions named and refused to commodify.**

## VERDICT 1 — Ostrom positioning (CONFIRMED, 3 narrowings)
All three legs hold (per `wf_a1807598-3d0`). Narrowings that make it reviewer-proof:
1. **Never "coequal" / "equals."** Ostrom: "Rarely do all the participants… have identical
   investment or harvesting power." Use **"bounded, repeatedly-interacting, heterogeneous
   appropriators who substantially share the resource's fate."** Do NOT claim "heterogeneity
   breaks Ostrom's cooperation" (that stronger claim was killed 0-3; the literature is mixed).
   The framework's domain is where the shared-fate structure BREAKS (capturer outside the set,
   or a dominated bearer). Foundational groundwater work (West Basin, depletable stock)
   confirms Ostrom's own domain is shared-fate.
2. **Power-critique citation = Mudliar & Koontz (2021), "Locating Power in Ostrom's Design
   Principles"** (principles "can conceal and perpetuate power asymmetries… even while
   improving environmental outcomes") + **Harvey** for the hard critique. Frame as "the
   principles under-theorize / can conceal power," NOT "Ostrom is power-blind." (Anthony &
   Campbell 2011 is a *sympathetic* internal critique — not the attack.)
3. **Cite-and-distinguish Costanza & Perrings (1990)** assurance bonding (see Residual ii).

## VERDICT 2 — Meta-claim #1 Polanyi/Fraser "diagnosed, never priced" (CONFIRMED, strengthened)
The tradition **resists monetization on principle** (per `wf_5002cc9c-317`):
- Fraser: commodifying land/nature is self-undermining, "a tiger that bites its own tail"
  (deployed AGAINST pricing). Expropriation defined as seizure "without paying" (the opposite
  of a price). [AEA 2023; Gunderson on the Polanyian counter-movement.]
- Eco-Marxist / metabolic-rift / unequal-ecological-exchange (Foster, Hornborg, Martínez-Alier
  via Neurath's incommensurability) operationalize in **non-monetary** metrics (emergy, ghost
  acres, ecological footprint) and reject price as the gauge on principle.
- Strongest potential defeater — Costanza's 1980/81 **energy theory of value** correlating
  ecological measure to dollars — **was repudiated from within** (Daly, Georgescu-Roegen,
  Odum; Costanza removed Odum from the *Ecological Economics* board, 1992). It was a
  price-correlation move, never a per-event severed-commons price.
- **REQUIRED FRAMING:** position the book's move as **cost-accounting to force honest
  reckoning, NOT commodification**; explicitly honor the tradition's refusal of the asset
  frame; claim the framework operationalizes their DIAGNOSIS in the cost-reckoning direction,
  against the commodification they opposed. (Ch5 L168/L172 already does this — keep + sharpen;
  do NOT claim Polanyi/Fraser would "endorse" pricing.)

## RESIDUAL PRIOR-ART THREADS (close-and-distinguish in the prose)
- **(i) NRDA (CERCLA/OPA):** CONFIRMED clean distinction — strictly **ex-post** (restoration
  owed after an already-occurred release; never a forward reserve). Book = **anticipatory**
  reserve at/before extraction. Make explicit.
- **(ii) Costanza & Perrings (1990):** the closest prior art — **anticipatory** (ex-ante,
  escrowed) like the framework, BUT the bond = **worst-case total potential damage**, not a
  per-event severed-residual-commons figure. Differentiator is the FIGURE, not the timing.
  **Cite-and-distinguish.**
- **(ii continued) Costanza-Perrings post-1990 lineage — CLOSED 2026-06-25 (CONFIRMED):** the
  lineage went two ways, NEITHER to per-event ecological value — the reclamation/financial-
  assurance branch (Gerard 2000; state assurance rules) bonds **physical cleanup cost**; the
  assurance branch stayed at **worst-case total potential damage**; Environmental Impact Bonds
  (DC Water etc., ~2016+) are pay-for-success green-infra financing. No extension re-anchors the
  bond to the per-event severed residual.
- **(iii) Hotelling user cost — CLOSED 2026-06-25 (CONFIRMED, novelty survives):** user cost /
  marginal scarcity rent prices the resource **owner's forgone future rent** (a private
  intertemporal opportunity cost in the optimal price path), NOT the third-party **severed
  commons value** (the externality/foreclosure tail); no standard construct fuses the two into a
  per-event reserved figure. **Distinguish:** owner's forgone rent vs. third-party severed
  commons value, reserved per event. (The book already uses Hotelling's scarcity-rent idea at
  the option-value component — consistent.)
- **(iv) Loss & Damage Fund — CLOSED 2026-06-25 (CONFIRMED, novelty survives):** the UNFCCC Fund
  for Responding to Loss and Damage pools **voluntary sovereign pledges** for **aggregate**
  climate assistance to vulnerable states; prices no single event, posts no per-event reserve,
  and was **deliberately structured to avoid per-emitter liability attribution**. Macro-aggregate
  analog (consistent with the H1 concession). **Distinguish:** pooled sovereign assistance vs.
  per-event severance price-and-reserve.
