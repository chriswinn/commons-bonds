## 0. Standing rules (author-ratified — apply to every piece)

1. **Lit-positioning is PRIMARY** (`feedback_lit_positioning_primacy.md`): every piece
   shows how the book supports / contradicts / adds to named adjacent literature; judges
   score positioning first, then flatness, then prose.
2. **Blind-draft-from-brief-only:** fresh drafters read the brief + ADDENDUM + named
   substrate ONLY — never the version on main or another venue. A missed beat is a BRIEF
   failure → amend the brief and re-draft; never graft on-main prose.
3. **MINIMUM-only, NEVER a maximum (ratified 2026-06-19; `feedback_substance_drives_length`).**
   A ceiling kills prose quality — it amputates beats. Set a floor only; substance drives
   length above it; try different minimums to see what beats surface. A venue word-limit
   is a LATER collaborative hand-edit-out, **never a drafting bound**. Empirical anchor:
   a 900-max on McDowell amputated ~40% of the argument; min-only ran to ~1,300–1,600w.
4. **Per-venue register dial (ratified 2026-06-19).** Tune to the venue's politics, not a
   default: **Noema** = confident-structural — lean INTO Polanyi/Fraser/Mazzucato, DROP
   the center-right hedging (post-ideological Berggruen; defensive preemptive-rebuttal
   *hurts* there). **Atlantic Ideas** = center-right; the disarming moves are dispositive.
   Op-eds = non-partisan cross-coalition.
5. **Ostrom corrected axis (ratified 2026-06-19).** Ostrom assumes homogeneity of interest
   (appropriators who share the commons's fate); the framework prices where that homogeneity
   FAILS / shared fate BREAKS — value-capturer outside the community OR internally-stratified
   dominant-vs-dominated (power + incentive). NOT "drawn-down vs managed-in-place"; NOT
   "permanent foreclosure" as a scope gate.
6. **Flatness watch + author redline is the final stage** on every piece.
7. **Hard rules:** zero em-dashes in candidate prose; no invented facts (structure-notes
   for gaps); figures trace to `manuscript/ledgers/_CANONICAL_FIGURE_LEDGER.md`; never a
   coercion/reparations dollar figure.
