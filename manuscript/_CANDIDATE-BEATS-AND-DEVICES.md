# Commons Bonds — Candidate Beats & Framing Devices (living, generative)

**Purpose.** The GENERATIVE companion to the analytical layers. The deep pass catalogued what is *there* and what to *cut/fix*; the landing-point map catalogued what must *land*. This file captures moves/beats/devices to **add** — bookend framings, recurring motifs, scene-anchors, structural arcs — at three scopes (book-wide / per-chapter / per-essay). Most begin as IDEAS to brainstorm. Lock only the ones that land.

**Lifecycle / status.** IDEA → IDEATED (options brainstormed) → DRAFTED (tried in prose) → **LANDED/LOCKED** (ratified, in the text) → or DISCARDED (drafted, didn't land — kept as a tried-and-cut record so it isn't re-proposed). Per the drafting doctrine: a candidate device gets a whole-cloth draft, you *see how it lands*, then lock or discard. Don't lock prematurely; don't lose the idea either.

**Connections.** Bookend/motif devices are setup→payoff loops, so a LOCKED one also registers in the coherence map. Devices should serve the through-question ("Am I looking at the full honest accounting?") and the spine, not decorate.
