## Ratified decisions

### Path B — Book first, essay paraphrases (chosen)

- Continue collecting personal stories until Ch 1 gaps fill.
- Once Ch 1 is more developed and starts coming together, pivot and knock out the revised essay using different phrasings of same lived material per Noema's "we only publish original content" rule.
- Author rationale: the parents-anchor passage (Candidate #6 letter) and other near-publisher-quality material are too good to lock into a 3,500-word essay where the book chapter then can't reuse the language. Book gets the canonical version; essay gets a paraphrase.
- Trade-off accepted: longer total timeline before resubmission; gain is that the book's most-vivid passages live in the book where they belong.

### Hook framing (e) — Chris-as-vignette plane scene at sunrise (chosen)

- Opener is the plane scene at sunrise per the Ch 1 sunrise/sunset bookend architecture (`Chapter__1___GuidanceDoc.md` §Sunrise/sunset bookend architecture, added 2026-05-01).
- Same vignette-with-specific-person pattern as the withdrawn essay's opener; replaces wife-medical-event with son-in-NICU + parents-aging displacement under the consent-rejection rewrite plan.
- Aligns with Ch 1's architectural decision so book + essay share scaffolding even though they cannot share sentences.
- Author rationale: McDowell County / Niger Delta / etc. travel for a someone-else-as-vignette opener is not feasible (time + resources); Chris-as-vignette preserves the personal-narrative-as-doorway-into-structural-argument architecture that worked in the withdrawn essay's structure.

### Interview commitment — YES, cold outreach to Mazzucato + Darity Jr. (chosen)

- Mariana Mazzucato (UCL Institute for Innovation and Public Purpose; *The Value of Everything*; *Mission Economy*) — adjacent framework intellectual; substantively engages cost severance + value-extraction territory.
- William Darity Jr. (Duke; reparations economics; *From Here to Equality* with A. Kirsten Mullen) — reparations-economics anchor; geographically near Hampton; approachable.
- Author commitment: comfortable with cold outreach to both.
- Adds 1-2 weeks of outreach + scheduling + writeup to the essay timeline; lifts the pitch's authority + adds primary-reporting layer to Section IV/V solutions material.

### Solutions cluster γ — Mazzucato + Mondragon + Vienna Gemeindebau + Ecuador or Whanganui (chosen by deference)

Multi-regional combination spanning:
- **Europe academic** — Mazzucato (UK; framework adjacency)
- **European cooperative** — Mondragon Corporation (Basque Spain; multi-generational existence proof beyond Norway; ~80,000 worker-owners across 95+ cooperatives)
- **European municipal** — Vienna Gemeindebau (Austrian; ~60% of Vienna's housing; century-plus operational record; survives interwar fascism + Nazi occupation + every postwar government)
- **Global South / indigenous-jurisdictional** — Ecuador rights-of-nature constitution (2008) OR Whanganui River legal personhood (NZ 2017)

Demonstrates framework's accountability-instrument architecture operating now in multiple modes: academic + cooperative + municipal + legal-jurisprudential. Multi-regional satisfies Noema's global-perspective preference. All four have strong source material in the book chapters (Ch 4 Norway; Ch 5 reparations economics; Ch 9 entering-wedges Mondragon + Vienna + Chattanooga + Alaska Permanent Fund; Ch 10 indigenous-related material). Reuse for essay = paraphrase per Path B.

### Target venue — Noema resubmit (chosen)

- Editorial fit confirmed strong by reading guidelines: solutions-over-diagnosis ✓; vignette-with-specific-person ✓; 2500-4000 words ✓; voice and color ✓.
- Layman's-terms requirement: not a problem. Author rationale: *"the book was very specifically more technical than the short essay as the essay could never get very technical with its length and the book had to get technical even if only in its appendix to have any chance of being taken seriously."* Essay revision under Path B is naturally accessible; technical apparatus stays in the book + Tech Appendix.
- Withdrawal was about consent, not editorial fit. Cold resubmit after substantive rewrite is plausible.
- AI statement remains thorough per existing pitch + Noema's AI guidelines requirement.

### Pivot-venue alternatives (preserved as backup)

- Other options held in reserve (Atlantic; Foreign Policy; Boston Review; Public Books; n+1) per editorial-profile fit.
