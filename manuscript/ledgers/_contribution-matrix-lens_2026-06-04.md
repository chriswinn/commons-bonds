## Meta-claims (sit on top of the whole matrix)
- STRUCTURAL-COMMONS / FICTITIOUS-COMMODITY META-CLAIM. The framework's distinctive move is to PRICE and RESERVE-against the treating-the-commons-as-extractable problem that Polanyi (fictitious commodities) and Fraser (expropriation of social reproduction and nature behind the wage relation) DIAGNOSED but never priced. Because a commons is defined structurally — anything depended-on and shared but not produced-as-a-commodity — a reef, the atmosphere, a community, a body of labor, and a future generation are the SAME OBJECT: a commons whose abundance masked its cost. The same apparatus (Four Gates + Three Ways of Counting) prices both, running FORWARD for ecological/atmospheric foreclosure (Foreclosure Bond B₂) and BACKWARD for realized human extraction / reparations (Restitution Bond B₁). RCV + the Accountability Bond are the pricing-and-accountability LAYER that the whole diagnostic tradition (Polanyi, Marx, Harvey, Mazzucato, Pistor, Christophers, and — by current omission — Fraser) shares an object with but never supplied. STATUS: the structural claim is built and ratified internally; the Polanyi naming is thin (not in the TA bibliography, fictitious-commodities never directly worked) and Fraser is entirely absent — making the strongest theoretical anchor for this very meta-claim the book's single biggest positioning gap.
- CONFIGURATIONAL, NOT SINGLE-MOVE, NOVELTY. The contribution is the integrated configuration applied to commons-extraction, not novelty in any one move: CIT discriminates (Lewis), Four Gates admit, Three Ways triangulate-and-estimate (Costanza / revealed preference / Dixit-Pindyck, Campbell-Fiske convergence), Two Instruments account bidirectionally (Hartwick+Parfit+civic-republican forward; Darity-Mullen backward), the Hotelling Identity (RCV − Hotelling rent = CS per unit) bridges to standard resource economics, Option C' accommodates political-philosophical diversity, and ARR (Savage) handles irreversibility. The framework EXTENDS its two parent traditions — Pigouvian externality theory and Ostrom commons-governance — into a setting neither addressed: extraction contexts where commons are drawn down rather than managed-in-place, including the non-renewable permanent-foreclosure commons (no harvest rate) and the heterogeneous/structurally-divided-stakeholder commons (via stratification economics), because the CIT operates on the EXTRACTION relationship, not the coordination relationship. The NYRB four-thinker synthesis is the crispest articulation: Pistor names the location, Mazzucato the activity, Christophers the mechanism, Susskind the metric — none operationalize the corrective at the per-extraction-event level; 'what remains is the assembly.'
- MEASUREMENT, NOT NEW DIAGNOSIS — AND CONSERVATIVE BY CONSTRUCTION. Against the critical/dispossession traditions (Harvey, Mazzucato, Federici, Patel & Moore, Coates) the value-add is the MAGNITUDE, not the mechanism: 'Mazzucato names the extraction; the framework adds the accounting' / 'Harvey diagnoses; RCV measures' / it 'gives the political analysis a number.' The credibility posture is deliberately conservative: adopting Weitzman declining discount rates biases RCV downward, and CS > 0 is shown robust across the entire Nordhaus-to-Stern rate range, with aggregate B < aggregate RCV established as a theorem (future generations cannot bid — extension of Coase). The framework converts an unresolvable philosophical question (the discount rate) into a tractable empirical one (substitutability), discloses parameter sensitivity rather than hiding behind a headline number, and is politically non-aligned by design: cost severance is a structural mechanism independent of ownership form (feudal, capitalist, state-owned, cooperative), reachable from regulatory caution, market discipline, or worker solidarity — honest pricing as 'the completion of the market Smith described.'

## Summary
The Commons Bonds contribution lens synthesizes four positioning reads (Technical Appendix, chapters 1/5/6/9/10, the nine-essay + two-op-ed corpus, and the structural-commons/political-economy rigor passes). The framework's spine is a single relationship move repeated across ~35 traditions: EXTEND-INTO-A-SETTING-THEY-COULD-NOT-REACH against the formal-economics canon (Pigou, Coase, Hotelling, Hartwick, Daly, Ostrom, Savage, Lewis, Dixit-Pindyck), ADD-TO against the diagnostic/critical traditions whose mechanism it prices rather than re-diagnoses (Harvey, Mazzucato, Pistor, Christophers, Darity-Mullen, Coates), PARALLEL for compatible-but-distinct frames it accommodates without absorbing (Public Choice, civic-republican, Sandel, Parfit, Raworth, Desmond), and CONTRADICT only for Hardin (sides with Ostrom). The signature formal claim is the Hotelling Identity (RCV − Hotelling rent = CS per unit); the signature meta-claim is structural-commons identity — ecological and human extraction are the same object priced forward (Foreclosure Bond) or backward (Restitution Bond), which is the operational descendant of Polanyi's fictitious-commodity thesis and Fraser's expropriation thesis. THREE GAPS are load-bearing for the deep passes: (1) Nancy Fraser is entirely absent yet is the closest living anchor for the central meta-claim (verified by grep — single biggest positioning gap); (2) Polanyi is thin (not in the TA bibliography, fictitious-commodities never directly worked); (3) the financialization/legal-coding objection (bonds extend the very Code-of-Capital logic the book critiques — Polanyi/Pistor/Christophers as OBJECTORS rather than allies) is un-engaged and is the strongest unanswered hostile line. Two further partials: Sandel's commodification critique is used as a convergent ally rather than rebutted as the commodification objection, and the conservative too-statist objection is named but not symmetrically rebutted the way the Marxist objection is. The framework is otherwise unusually systematic about steelmanning (the discount-rate, ESG-capture, overcounting, Ostrom-commodification, and Norway-non-transferability objections all land well). Most chapters carry the positioning more thinly than the essays — NYRB's four-thinker 'assembly' synthesis and $100 Barrel's 'lineage of near-misses' are the crispest articulations and should seed the chapter deep passes.
