## Meta-claims (sit on top of the whole matrix)

- **STRUCTURAL-COMMONS / FICTITIOUS-COMMODITY META-CLAIM.** The framework's
  distinctive move is to PRICE and RESERVE-against the treating-the-commons-as-
  extractable problem that Polanyi (fictitious commodities) and Fraser
  (expropriation of social reproduction and nature behind the wage relation)
  DIAGNOSED but never priced. Because a commons is defined structurally —
  anything depended-on and shared but not produced-as-a-commodity — a reef, the
  atmosphere, a community, a body of labor, and a future generation are the
  SAME OBJECT: a commons whose abundance masked its cost. The same apparatus
  (Four Gates + Three Ways of Counting) prices both, running FORWARD for
  ecological/atmospheric foreclosure (Foreclosure Bond B₂) and BACKWARD for
  realized human extraction / reparations (Restitution Bond B₁).
  **CURRENT STATUS (v2): the same-object/same-shape SEED lands narratively in
  Ch1 (L52 "same shape"), Ch2 (L35/L213 "same sentence... same structure"), and
  Ch3 (L236/L242 "the rhyme is the proof"); the structural commons-DEFINITION +
  the Polanyi/Fraser anchor are FULLY worked only in Ch5 (L150-166 "The same
  object, named honestly"). Polanyi/Fraser remain MISSING from Ch1, Ch3, Ch6,
  Ch7, Ch8, Ch10 and (canonically) Ch9. This is still the book's single biggest
  positioning gap by breadth, even though Ch5 now carries the marquee fill.**

- **CONFIGURATIONAL, NOT SINGLE-MOVE, NOVELTY.** The contribution is the
  integrated configuration applied to commons-extraction, not novelty in any one
  move: CIT discriminates (Lewis), Four Gates admit, Three Ways triangulate
  (Costanza / revealed preference / Dixit-Pindyck, Campbell-Fiske convergence),
  Two Instruments account bidirectionally (Hartwick+Parfit+civic-republican
  forward; Darity-Mullen backward), the Hotelling Identity (RCV − Hotelling rent
  = CS per unit) bridges to standard resource economics, Option C′ accommodates
  political-philosophical diversity, and ARR (Savage) handles irreversibility.
  The framework EXTENDS its two parent traditions — Pigouvian externality theory
  and Ostrom commons-governance — into a setting neither addressed. The NYRB
  four-thinker synthesis is the crispest articulation: Pistor names the
  location, Mazzucato the activity, Christophers the mechanism, Susskind the
  metric — none operationalize the corrective at the per-extraction-event level;
  "what remains is the assembly."
  **CURRENT STATUS (v2): the configurational spine is delivered across the
  formal-economics chapters — Ch6 carries the full Three Ways + Four Gates
  apparatus (17/23 matrix points LANDED per audit); Ch9 carries the
  property-rights-limits + instrument-design assembly (10-11/16 LANDED). The
  NYRB four-thinker assembly remains an ESSAY-side articulation; Susskind is
  MISSING from canonical chapter prose.**

- **MEASUREMENT, NOT NEW DIAGNOSIS — AND CONSERVATIVE BY CONSTRUCTION.**
  Against the critical/dispossession traditions (Harvey, Mazzucato, Federici,
  Patel & Moore, Coates) the value-add is the MAGNITUDE, not the mechanism:
  "Mazzucato names the extraction; the framework adds the accounting" / "Harvey
  diagnoses; RCV measures." Conservative posture: Weitzman declining discount
  rates bias RCV downward; CS > 0 robust across the Nordhaus-to-Stern range;
  aggregate B < aggregate RCV is a theorem.
  **CURRENT STATUS (v2): the measurement-not-diagnosis posture is ENACTED most
  fully in Ch2 (L173/L183/L185 "the missing instrument") and Ch8 (the single
  worked 8-component ton) — but in BOTH, the named critical-tradition thinkers
  (Harvey, Mazzucato, Pistor) are absent from running prose ("matrix work with
  zero attribution"). The attribution lands in Ch5/Ch6/Ch9; the magnitude-work
  lands in Ch2/Ch3/Ch8.**
