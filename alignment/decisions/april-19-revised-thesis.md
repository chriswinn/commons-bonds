The thesis was stress-tested across six cases in the April 19 session and held in every instance. The revised formulation using *commons bonds* as the active concept:

> Every extractive system in human history has relied on the same mechanism — creating commons bonds and immediately severing them — and no political or economic framework has yet built the institutional structure to make those bonds permanent.

Compare to the prior version (*capturing value while severing accountability*). The new formulation is more concrete, more relational, and more morally immediate. You can feel a bond. You can feel it sever.
