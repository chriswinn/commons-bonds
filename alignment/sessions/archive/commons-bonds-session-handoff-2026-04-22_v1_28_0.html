Container term: "layers" → "dimensions" (canonical)

Meta-term rigor pass surfaced that "layers" has two problems: (a) structural imprecision — geological/archaeological stacking metaphor implies hierarchy, which is structurally false for an additive/compositional framework per §5.4 single-layer cost severance; (b) literal collision on Atmospheric (layer-of-atmosphere is a physics meaning). "Dimensions" wins cleanly against five alternatives (axes, primitives, domains, planes, fields):

  • Axes fails on three hard collisions (Geographic/Spatial coordinate-axis, Political left-right-spectrum, Cosmic rotational-axis) plus framework-internal overload with "scarcity axis" methodology language.
  • Primitives disqualified on bilateral cross-spectrum attack surface (colonialist residue, flagged by both left and right critics) plus term collision with §5.3 primitiveness gate.
  • Domains / Planes / Fields middle-performing, not dominant on any test.
  • Dimensions passes structural-accuracy (independence + additivity), strongest Test 20/23 compression, most accessible at Character 11 (organizer) and Character 10 (labor-lawyer), positive Test 18 overlap via Sen/Nussbaum/Raworth welfare-dimensions + Hofstede cultural-dimensions.

Commons Bonds operating core (canonical — updated v1.28.0)

AIT positioning (canonical, from C9 patch §3)

The Abundance Interference Taxonomy is the framework's epistemic core. Dimensions are organizational scaffolding, not load-bearing structure. Dimension additions, mergers, or renames are organizational revisions, not framework-integrity events.

Canonical container term: "dimensions" (v1.28.0)

Replaces "layers" per meta-term rigor pass this session. Historical docs retain "layers" as lineage; canonical usage is "dimensions" effective v1.28.0. Rationale: (a) structural accuracy — independence + additivity; (b) no literal collisions; (c) strongest Test 20/23 compression; (d) positive Test 18 overlap via Sen/Nussbaum/Raworth welfare-dimensions + Hofstede cultural-dimensions; (e) accessible at organizer + worker register.

Canonical abundance-dimension set (10 dimensions; Book One canonical)

Atmospheric→Habitability (locked) · Geographic→Spatial (Priority-2) · Temporal (stable) · Institutional (stable) · Demographic→Kinship-or-Belonging (OPEN DECISION) · Ecological→Ecosystem (locked, pair with Habitability) · Political (stable, US-partisan watch) · Cohesion→Social-Fabric / Civic-Ties (Priority-2) · Informational→Evidentiary (Priority-2) · Agency→Autonomy (Priority-2). No 11th dimension. Availability-absence is severity-marker across the 10 (methodology §5.5), not a primitive axis.

Two-path rigor test — apply before ratifying any architectural claim

  • Path 1 — Allocation symmetry: Is the structural value-capture / cost-bearing asymmetry present?
  • Path 2 — Shield absence: Is the distance condition absent? Shield typology: epistemic, institutional, political, fiduciary, spatial, temporal, consent-normalization (v1.2.1).

Both paths must confirm. Structural allocation is the mechanism of cost severance. Distance is the shielding condition.

Merger gate — apply before ratifying any new or renamed dimension

Demonstrate bilateral independence with cases in both directions. Habitability ⊥ Ecosystem example this session: engineered sterile habitats have habitability without ecosystem; extremophile communities have ecosystem without human-habitability. If independence fails in either direction, merger is indicated.

Primitiveness gate — apply before ratifying any new dimension

The dimension's revealed costs must not decompose cleanly into compositions of existing dimensions. If they do, the candidate is a cross-dimension concept, not a new dimension. Availability-absence (v1.28.0) is the canonical worked example of primitiveness-gate failure — appeared to be a distinct axis (Irreversibility / Substrate / Cosmic) but revealed on stress-test as a severity-property across existing dimensions.

Single-dimension cost severance (v1.27.0; renamed v1.28.0)

The framework is additive and compositional. A case activating one dimension is a full instance of cost severance — multi-dimension activation is not required. Goal-2 direct-contribution work often succeeds at single-dimension precision. See abundance dimensions doc §5.4.

Availability-absence as severity-marker (v1.28.0 NEW)

Every dimension prices availability-absence of its specific thing (Atmospheric: clean air; Temporal: future compensation; Ecological: ecosystem services; etc.). Allocation choices that make things permanently unavailable within the framework's operating horizon push costs on existing dimensions to their extreme — a severity property, not a primitive axis. See abundance dimensions doc §5.5 (proposed, to be added next session).

Canonical cases are dimension-inherent (v1.26.0; lived-experience emphasis v1.28.0)

A dimension's canonical cases are not incidental — they are what the dimension means in practice. Career-risk, legal exposure, and reception pressures route through specific dimensions via their canonical cases. When lived-experience cases are available (Chris's Story 1, Story 2, 120-hour-week, NYC consulting years, Appalachian roots), foreground them in cohort and chapter intros — they do canonical-case work thought-experiment constructions cannot.

Revisitability

Rejected candidates remain revisitable. A candidate concept that fails one framing may succeed under another as understanding sharpens. Failure is a finding about the tested form, not a permanent verdict. Availability-absence-as-dimension failed v1.28.0 stress-test; revisitable if a future primitive candidate surfaces that doesn't collapse the same way.
