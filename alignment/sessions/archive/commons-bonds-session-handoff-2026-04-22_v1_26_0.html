Canonical additions and corrections this session

Shield typology — consent-normalization (added v1.2.1 §5.2)

Individual consent, when treated by the surrounding social, legal, and institutional framework as terminally closing the accounting — "they chose it, no one owes them anything" — operates as a structural social shield even when individual awareness is complete. The shield is not at the individual-recognition level; it operates at social-accounting terminus. Canonical case: a knowledge worker who knowingly traded personal life for 120+ hour weeks is not shield-absent by virtue of awareness; the shield is the framework that treats their consent as closing the owing. Applies particularly to Institutional, Temporal, and Agency layer cases. Full treatment in rigor pass record §5.

Test 13 correction — career-risk is layer-inherent via canonical cases (v1.2.1 §7)

The original v1.2.0 framing implied Test 13 (career-risk compatibility) was layer-indifferent. It is not. Career-risk does real work at the layer level when a layer's canonical cases involve industries capable of professional retaliation: Informational (tobacco-industry internal-research suppression, pharma data suppression, opioid-death geography mapped to Appalachian counties), Political (regulatory capture by industries whose lobbying networks interlock with healthcare financing), and secondarily Institutional (CEO-era case content, partially addressed by pre-drafting NDA and anonymization gates). A layer's canonical cases are not incidental to the layer — they are what the layer means in practice. Mitigation is case-level presentation / framing, not layer-level.

Commons Bonds operating core (canonical — updated v1.26.0)

AIT positioning (canonical, from C9 patch §3)

The Abundance Interference Taxonomy is the framework's epistemic core. Layers are organizational scaffolding, not load-bearing structure. Layer additions, mergers, or renames are organizational revisions, not framework-integrity events. The AIT operates on any cost-scarcity relationship independently of a containing taxonomy — its validity is a property of the cost-scarcity relationship itself, not of any layer set.

Canonical abundance-layer set (10 layers, per v1.21.0; awaiting Part 2 gap-close under v1.2.1)

Atmospheric (rename pending) · Geographic (rename pending) · Temporal · Institutional · Demographic (rename pending) · Ecological · Political · Cohesion (name TBD) · Informational (name TBD) · Agency (name TBD). See roster table above.

Two-path rigor test — apply before ratifying any architectural claim

  • Path 1 — Allocation symmetry: Is the structural value-capture / cost-bearing asymmetry present?
  • Path 2 — Shield absence: Is the distance condition (the shielding mechanism) absent? Shield typology: epistemic, institutional, political, fiduciary, spatial, temporal, consent-normalization (added v1.2.1).

Both paths must confirm. Structural allocation is the mechanism of cost severance. Distance is the shielding condition. Individual awareness and agreement do not dissolve cost severance when the social framework treats consent as terminally closing the accounting.

Merger gate — apply before ratifying any new or renamed layer

Demonstrate bilateral independence with cases in both directions. If independence fails in either direction, merger is indicated.

Primitiveness gate — apply before ratifying any new layer

The layer's revealed costs must not decompose cleanly into compositions of existing layers. If they do, the candidate is a cross-layer concept, not a new layer.

Canonical cases are layer-inherent (added v1.26.0)

A layer's canonical cases are not incidental to the layer; they are what the layer means in practice. Career-risk, legal exposure, and reception pressures route through specific layers via their canonical cases — layer-indifferent-in-the-abstract does not mean layer-indifferent-in-practice. Verified pattern as of the Test 13 correction.

Revisitability

Rejected candidates remain revisitable. A candidate concept that fails one framing may succeed under another as understanding sharpens. Failure is a finding about the tested form, not a permanent verdict on the concept space.

Operating discipline

"Push harder so we only rewrite once." Applies to layer set, protocol, AIT positioning, chapter framing, book scope, and the rigor protocol's own lineage.
