- **KEEP-CANONICAL** — defensible and fully acceptable, but canonical carries 12 em-dashes (gate
  violation the candidates fix to 0), lacks the author's own family-standing material (Darling
  shell history, the driveway beat, the favorite-fish loss, the deadly-weight clothing) that the
  06-06 author-edits supply, and still needs the reef section. B gives all of canonical's
  framework-integration strength PLUS the author-edit material PLUS the em-dash fix.
- **PROMOTE-A** — A is the better *scene* draft and the strongest single candidate on vitality;
  it loses to B only on length discipline (+867 w over B) and a few self-narrating closes. A
  legitimate alternative if the author weights scene-richness over leanness; the fold-in list
  would invert (promote A, fold in B's close-discipline).
- **PROMOTE-V2-WHOLECLOTH** — v2 has the best hook, the leanest body, and is the ONLY text with
  the reef section already drafted, which is a real argument for it. But it (a) carries the
  FACT-CORRECTION-falsified boardwalk beat (must be fixed pre-ship), (b) introduces an
  unverified Chris Moore quote, (c) asserts the unverified 1881 Darling founding, and (d) is a
  wholly different chapter architecture (love-the-water-first; "let me add up the column") that
  would be a larger structural commitment than promoting a canonical-lineage text. Acceptable if
  the author prefers v2's architecture, but it trades three fact-exposures back in for the reef
  section that can instead be ported cleanly onto post-correction B.
