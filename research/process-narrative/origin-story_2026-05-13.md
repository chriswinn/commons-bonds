## The narrative (author's words, lightly cleaned)

I had an idea as I was reading about *accumulation by dispossession*, and then I thought I saw it everywhere in the world — not just in capitalist societies. Then I noticed how much it just felt dishonest.

So I started conversations with past sessions with Claude in March 2026. Those conversations touched on lifetimes of focused career knowledge that I have never touched or been exposed to. Claude simply told me the work that was adjacent, and synthesized how it was related to what I was working on.

Then I said: we should step back and calculate the actual cost that was severed.

That eventually led to the $100 barrel of oil and the coal extraction calculations. And the asteroid-miner scenario I daydreamed.

Then Claude ran that calculation on every single commons across the world, and I realized this was bigger than me. So we started planning on how I could try to effect change as one person. The most likely route is to make what is currently invisible, visible. And along that path I should give words that help people in these conversations.

I have absolutely leveraged Claude extensively throughout this effort.

8. **"Leveraged extensively"** — full honesty about scope of use. Not a casual tool, a durable collaborator.
